Item a.
The first statement is incorrect. There is no law that says \((a+b)^n\) is equivalent to \(a^n+b^n\text{,}\) so \((1+r)^4\) is not equivalent to \(1^4+r^4\) or \(1+r^4\text{.}\) However, it is true that \((ab)^n = a^nb^n\text{,}\) so in particular \((ab^t)^3 = a^3(b^t)^3 = a^3b^{3t}\text{.}\)
