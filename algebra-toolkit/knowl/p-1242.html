We can make this conjecture more plausible by examining the formula for the \(n^{\text{th}}\) partial sum of a geometric series,

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] S_n\amp = \dfrac{a_{n+1} - a_1}{r-1}\\ \amp = \dfrac{ar^n - a}{r-1} = \dfrac{a - ar^n}{1-r}~~~~~~~~~~~~~(r \not= 1)\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
