Solve by factoring. Get zero on one side of the equation.

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] 3x^2-12x \amp = 0 \amp \amp \blert{\text{Factor the left side.}}\\ 3x(x-4) \amp = 0 \amp \amp \blert{\text{Set each factor equal to zero.}}\\ 3x=0,~~~~x-4 \amp = 0 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

The solutions are \(x=0\) and \(x=4\text{.}\)
