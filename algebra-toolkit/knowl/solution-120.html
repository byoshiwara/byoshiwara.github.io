Solution 6.18.1.

Solve by factoring. Factor the left side.

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] (x-4)(3x+2) \amp = 0 \amp \amp \blert{\text{Set each factor equal to zero.}}\\ x-4=0,~~~~3x+2 \amp = 0 \amp \amp \blert{\text{Solve each equation.}}\\ x=4,~~~~x\amp = \dfrac{-2}{3} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

The solutions are \(x=0\) and \(x=\dfrac{-2}{3}\text{.}\)
