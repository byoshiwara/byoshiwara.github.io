This time we take the expansion

\begin{equation*} (a+b)^4 = a^4 + 4a^3b + 6a^2b^2 + 4ab^3 + b^4 \end{equation*}

from the Investigation and let \(a=2m\) and \(b = -n\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] (2m-n)^4 \amp = (2m)^4 + 4(2m)^3(-n) + 6(2m)^2(-n)^2 + 4(2m)(-n)^3 + (-n)^4\\ \amp = 16m^4 + 4(8m^3)(-n) + 6(4m^2)(n^2) + 4(2m)(-n^3) + n^4 \\ \amp = 16m^4 - 32m^3n + 24m^2n^2 - 8mn^3 + n^4 \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
