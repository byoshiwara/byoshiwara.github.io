Use the formula \(~(a-b)^2=a^2-2ab+b^2~\) with \(~a=\dfrac{i\sqrt{3}}{2}~\) and \(~b=\dfrac{1}{2}.~\)

\begin{align*} (a-b)^2 \amp = a^2-2ab+b^2\\ (\dfrac{i\sqrt{3}}{2}-\dfrac{1}{2})^2 \amp = (\dfrac{i\sqrt{3}}{2})^2-2(\dfrac{i\sqrt{3}}{2})(\dfrac{1}{2})+(\dfrac{1}{2})^2\\ \amp = \dfrac{-3}{4}-\dfrac{i\sqrt{3}}{2}+\dfrac{1}{4}\\ \amp = \dfrac{-1}{2}-\dfrac{i\sqrt{3}}{2} \end{align*}
