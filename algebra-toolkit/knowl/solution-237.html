Solution 9.57.1.

  1. \(_4C_3\) is the coefficient of the term containing \(b^3\) in the expansion of \((a+b)^4\text{.}\) Referring to Step 5 in the Investigation, we see that the coefficient of the term \(4ab^3\) is 4. Thus, \(_4C_3 = 4\text{.}\)

  2. \(_5C_2\) is the coefficient of the term containing \(b^2\) in the expansion of \((a+b)^5\text{.}\) Referring to Step 6 in the Investigation, we see that the coefficient of the term \(10a^3b^2\) is 10. Thus, \(_5C_2 = 10\text{.}\)
