Solution 6.23.1.

  1. We can divide out a factor of 2.

    \begin{equation*} \dfrac{3}{8} \cdot \dfrac{6}{5} = \dfrac{3}{\cancel{2} \cdot 4} \cdot \dfrac{\cancel{2} \cdot 3}{5} = \dfrac{9}{20} \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \dfrac{ab}{6} \cdot \dfrac{3a}{2b} = \dfrac{a \cancel{b}}{2 \cdot \cancel{3}} \cdot \dfrac{\cancel{3}a}{2 \cancel{b}} = \dfrac{a^2}{4} \end{equation*}
