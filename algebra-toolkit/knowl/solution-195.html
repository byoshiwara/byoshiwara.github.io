Solution 7.39.1.

We factor each numerator and denominator, and look for common factors.

\begin{align*} \dfrac{4y^2-1}{4-y^2} \cdot \dfrac{y^2-2y}{4y+2} \amp = \dfrac{(2y-1)\cancel{(2y+1)}}{\cancel{(2-y)}(2+y)} \cdot \dfrac{y(-1)\cancel{(y-2)}}{2\cancel{(2y+1)}} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Divide out common factors.}}\\ \amp = \dfrac{-y(2y-1)}{2(y+2)} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Note:}~~y-2=-(2-y)} \end{align*}
