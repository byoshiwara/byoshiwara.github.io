Finally, we write the trinomial as \(~8x^2-24x+3x-9~\text{,}\) and factor by grouping:

\begin{align*} (8x^2-24x)+(3x-9)~~~~~~~~ \amp \blert{\text{Factor out the greatest common factor from each group.}}\\ 8x\alert{(x-3)}+3\alert{(x-3)}~~~~~~~~ \amp \blert{\text{Factor out the common binomial.}}\\ (x-3)(8x+3)~~~~~~~~ \end{align*}

Thus, \(~8x^2-21x-9 = (x-3)(8x+3).\)
