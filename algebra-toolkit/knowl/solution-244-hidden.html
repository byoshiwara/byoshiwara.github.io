Start by writing down the expansion of \((a+b)^6\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} (a+b)^6 = a^6 + \underline{\hspace{1.818181818181818em}}a^5b+\underline{\hspace{1.818181818181818em}}a^4b^2+\underline{\hspace{1.818181818181818em}}a^3b^3+\underline{\hspace{1.818181818181818em}}+a^2b^4+\underline{\hspace{1.818181818181818em}}ab^5+b^6 \end{equation*}

Next, fill in the blanks with the binomial coefficients from Pascal's triangle.

\begin{equation*} (a+b)^6 = a^6 + 6a^5b+15a^4b^2+20a^3b^3+15a^2b^4+6ab^5+b^6 \end{equation*}
