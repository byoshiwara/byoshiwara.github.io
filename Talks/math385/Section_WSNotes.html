Section Workshop Notes

The following is a possible plan for a two-hour workshop. If you have some additional time, include time for a discussion of Math Gateway. If you have less time, just focus on one of MathDL and Math Gateway.

Timetable

  • 10 minutes: Introductions, review of schedule

  • 10 minutes: Intro to MathDL

  • 30 minutes: Explore MathDL

  • 15 minutes: Discussion

  • 10 minutes: Intro to Math Gateway

  • 30 minutes: Explore Math Gateway

  • 15 minutes: Evaluation

Resources

  • There is a $500 stipend for each presenter.

  • There is up to $500 per section workshop available to help with technical issues.

  • We will provide online workshop registration (at MAA).

  • We will create a web site for each section workshop with a list of participants, schedule, resources, links to online evaluation forms and other materials provided by the presenter.

  • We will create a Workshop List Serve for each workshop.

  • We will provide 4-page Math Gateway handouts.

  • We will provide NSDL handouts.

  • Within the workshop web site, we will give links to forms for people who would like to

    • Review for MAA Reviews
    • Review for DCR
    • Review for Convergence
    • Referee for JOMA
    • Suggest a Math Gateway partner
