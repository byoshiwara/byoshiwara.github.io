CALIFORNIA ONLINE MATHEMATICS EDUCATION TIMES (COMET)
California Online Mathematics Education Times (COMET) is a weekly news journal designed for mathematics educators and educational leaders. COMET features useful and timely information on current educational issues, web resources, professional events and opportunities, and news articles from California and across the nation. COMET is produced by Carol Fry Bohlin (carolb@csufresno.edu; 559-278-0237).
