What is MathDL?

The MAA Mathematical Sciences Digital Library is the mathematics branch of the National Science Digital Library (NSDL, a.k.a. National STEM Distributed Learning).

MathDL includes resources hosted by the Mathematical Association of America, such as its online journal Loci, and a search engine over its partner organizations.

Loci incorporates what historically had been three separate electronic entities within MathDL: Convergence (which focused on bringing history into the mathematics classroom, including problems from history, quotations, and "On This Day"), JOMA (Journal of Online Mathematics and its Applications), and Digital Classroom Resources.
