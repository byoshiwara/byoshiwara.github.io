Automatic updates: RSS feeds and blogs

Math in the News, On This Day, and Loci Featured Items have RSS feeds. This means you can have updates appear automatically within a webpage or Learning Management System (like Moodle, MyMathLab, Etudes, BlackBoard, WebCT) by embedding the proper code.

Also many of the columns on the MAA homepage have RSS feeds.

Number a Day and Minute Math are both blogs, so you can subscribe for automatic updates.
