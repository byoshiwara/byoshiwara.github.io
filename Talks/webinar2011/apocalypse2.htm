Apocalypse, (Greek: Ἀποκάλυψις Apokálypsis; "lifting of the veil" or "revelation"), disclosure to certain privileged persons of something hidden from the majority of humankind

It appears that Wolfram|Alpha is unknown to the majority of mankind, so in an arcane sense, there has been an apocalypse regarding Wolfram|Alpha.

Despite the initial fanfare, Wolfram|Alpha has not made a dramatic change in education.

But as responsible educators, we should be helping our students (and colleagues) to learn how to take advantage of the power that Wolfram|Alpha provides.
