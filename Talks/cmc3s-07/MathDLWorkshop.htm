Where can I find great lessons, class activities, projects, and computer mathlets?

Doubletree Hotel Anaheim/Orange County

March 2, 2007

This workshop is designed to inform participants about the Mathematical Sciences Digital Library (MathDL) and the MAA Math Gateway project.

The workshop will be conducted by Bruce Yoshiwara (Los Angeles Pierce College). Major support is being provided by the MAA (through the National Science Digital Library Program), and facilities were arranged by CMC3-South.
