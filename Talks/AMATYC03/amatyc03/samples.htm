Samples and Links

I have found a few web sites with nice examples of interactive lessons using spreadsheets.

Rob Kimball's Using Excel to Excite has many fully developed modules used at Wake Forest for their Exemplary Program in technical mathematics.

Sarah Mabrouk has created several nice spreadsheets appropriate for both precalculus and calculus (as well as Maple worksheets).

R. Narasimhan has Excel worksheets for finite math, college algebra, applied calculus, and calculus.

Online Journals

Here are two relevant online journals. The Journal of Online Mathematics and its Applilcations (JOMA) includes spreadsheet applications among its many online resources. The Spreadsheets in Education Journal involves all disciplines, and does not focus on mathematics.
