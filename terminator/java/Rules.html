The object of the game

At the start of the game, a Terminator will appear at a random position on the screen. Your object is to Terminate the Terminator!
To destroy the Terminator, you must determine the proper firing range and angle (in radians) of fire. A circle shows the range that you have designated, and the message "Angle lock is on!" will appear when the weapon angle is close to correct. Notice the weapon begins with a range of 2.5 and an angle of 1 radian.

Press the "Fire" button. If the attack coordinates are accurate, you'll terminate the terminator! If not, you'll see where the weapon did fire, and you should be able to adjust the range or angle appropriately before firing again.

When you terminate the Terminator, enjoy the results, then click on the "Reset" button for a new random attacker.

Tricks

You have the ability to fire backwards (or "over the shoulder") to catch the Terminator off guard. Aim the weapon exactly opposite the direction of the Terminator. (Look for the "Back Angle lock" message.) Use a negative value for range, and the weapon will fire backwards.
