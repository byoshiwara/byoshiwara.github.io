Technology1.67Using a Calculator to Graph a Function

We can also use a graphing calculator to obtain a table and graph for the function in Example 1.65. We graph a function just as we graphed an equation. For this function, we enter \(Y_1 = \sqrt{~^~}(X+4)\) and press ZOOM \(6\) for the standard window. The calculator’s graph is shown in Figure 1.68.
