Use the graph to solve the equation or inequality

21

Refer to the graph of \(y = \abs{\dfrac{x}{2}-1}\)

  1. Solve \(\abs{\dfrac{x}{2}-1}=2\)

  2. Solve \(\abs{\dfrac{x}{2}-1}\lt 2\)

  3. Solve \(\abs{\dfrac{x}{2}-1}\ge 2\)

22

Refer to the graph of \(y = \dfrac{-x^2}{2}+x+1\)

  1. Solve \(\dfrac{-x^2}{2}+x+1=-3\)

  2. Solve \(\dfrac{-x^2}{2}+x+1\ge -3\)

  3. Solve \(\dfrac{-x^2}{2}+x+1\le -3\)

23

Refer to the graph of \(y = \dfrac{6}{x^2-3x+3}\)

  1. Solve \(2=\dfrac{6}{x^2-3x+3}\)

  2. Solve \(2\gt\dfrac{6}{x^2-3x+3}\)

  3. Solve \(2\lt\dfrac{6}{x^2-3x+3}\)

  2. Solve \(8\gt\abs{x^3 + 3x^2 + 3x +1}\)
