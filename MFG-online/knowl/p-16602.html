We multiply the numerator and denominator of the first fraction by \((x - 3)\) to obtain an equivalent fraction:

\begin{equation*} \frac{3x}{x+2}=\frac{3x\alert{(x-3)}}{(x+2)\alert{(x-3)}}=\frac{3x^2-9x}{x^2-x-6} \end{equation*}
