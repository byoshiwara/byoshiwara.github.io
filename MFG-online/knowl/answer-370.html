  1. Reflected about the \(v\)-axis and stretched vertically by a factor of \(2\text{:}\) \(T = -2h(v)\)

  2. Stretched vertically by a factor of \(3\text{:}\) \(T = 3h(v)\)

  3. Translated \(3\) units up: \(T = h(v) + 3\)

  4. Translated \(3\) units left: \(T = h(v + 3)\)
