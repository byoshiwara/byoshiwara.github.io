The Sierpinski carpet is another fractal. It is named for the Polish mathematician Waclaw Sierpinski (1882â€“1969). Here is how to build a Sierpinski carpet:

  • Start with a unit square (sides of length 1 unit.)
  • For stage \(n = 1\text{,}\) trisect each side and partition the square into 9 smaller squares. Remove the center square, leaving a hole surrounded by 8 squares, as shown in the figure.
  • For stage \(n = 2\text{,}\) repeat the process on each of the remaining 8 squares.
  • If you continue this process forever, the resulting is the Sierpinski carpet.
