\begin{equation*} I (t) = I_0 e^{-1.88t} \end{equation*}

where \(I_0\) is the X-ray count at the source and \(I (t)\) is the X-ray count behind a lead plate of thickness \(t\) inches.

  1. Graph \(I (t)\text{.}\)

  2. What percent of an X-ray beam will penetrate a lead plate \(12\) inch thick?

  3. How thick should the lead plate be in order to screen out \(70\%\) of the X-rays?
