The index of the radical is \(3\text{,}\) so we look for perfect cube factors of \(24x^6 y^8\text{.}\) The factor \(8\) is a perfect cube, and \(x^6\) and \(y^6\) have exponents divisible by \(3\text{.}\) Thus,

\begin{align*} \sqrt[3]{24x^6y^8} \amp = \sqrt[3]{8x^6 y^6 \cdot 3 y^2}\amp\amp\text{Apply Property (1).}\\ \amp = \sqrt[3]{8x^6 y^6} \sqrt[3]{3 y^2}\amp\amp\text{Take cube roots.}\\ \amp = 2x^2 y^2\sqrt[3]{3y^2} \end{align*}
