Recall that the graph of \(y = -f (x)\) is the reflection about the \(x\)-axis of the graph of \(y = f (x)\text{.}\) The graphs of \(y = 2^x\) and \(y = -2^x\) are shown in Figure 4.32. You may have also noticed a relationship between the graphs of \(f (x) = 2^x\) and \(g(x) = \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^x\text{,}\) which are shown in Figure 4.33.
