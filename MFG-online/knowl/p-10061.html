Choose the graph for each function described below.

  1. The area, \(A\text{,}\) of a pentagon is a quadratic function of the length, \(l\text{,}\) of its side.

  2. The strength, \(F\text{,}\) of a hurricane varies inversely with its speed, \(s\text{.}\)

  3. The price of food has increased by \(3\%\) every year for a decade.

  4. The magnitude, \(M\text{,}\) of a star is a logarithmic function of its brightness, \(I\text{.}\)

  5. The speed of the train increased at a constant rate.

  6. If you do not practice a foreign language, you lose \(\frac{1}{8} \) of the words in your working vocabulary, \(V\text{,}\) each year.
