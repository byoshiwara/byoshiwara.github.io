Exercise 27

The average daily high temperature in Fairbanks, Alaska can be approximated by a sinusoidal function with a period of 12 months. The low temperature of \(-1.6\degree\) occurs in January, and the high temperature of \(72.3\degree\) in July.

  1. What are the midline, period, and amplitude?
  2. Write a formula for the average daily high temperature \(T(m)\text{,}\) where \(m\) is the number of months since January.
  3. Graph \(T(m)\) for two periods, labeling the points that correspond to highest and lowest average temperature.
