Then we apply the formula for \(\cos (\theta)\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] \cos (\theta) \amp = \dfrac{{\bf{v}} \cdot {\bf{w}}}{\|{\bf{v}}\| \|{\bf{w}}\|} = \dfrac{-9}{\sqrt{13}\sqrt{34}} = -0.4281\\ \theta \amp = \cos^{-1}(-0.4281) = 115.35\degree\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
The angle between the vectors is \(115.35\degree\text{.}\)
