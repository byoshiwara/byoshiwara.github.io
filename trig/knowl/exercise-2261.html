Exercise 31.

When a plane is tilted by an angle \(\theta\) from the horizontal, the time required for a ball starting from rest to roll a horizontal distance of \(l\) feet on the plane is

\begin{equation*} t=\sqrt{\dfrac{l}{8}\csc(2\theta)}~~ \text{seconds} \end{equation*}
  1. How long, to the nearest 0.01 second, will it take the ball to roll 2 feet horizontally on a plane tilted by \(12\degree\text{?}\)
  2. Solve the formula for \(l\) in terms of \(t\) and \(\theta\text{.}\)
