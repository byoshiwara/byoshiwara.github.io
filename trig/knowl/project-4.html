Algebra Refresher 2.2.

Solve the inequality.
1. \(\:6-x \gt 3\)
2. \(\:\dfrac{-3x}{4} \ge -6\)
3. \(\:3x-7 \le -10\)
4. \(\:4-3x \lt 2x+9\)
If \(x \lt 0\text{,}\) which of the following expressions are positive, and which are negative?
5. \(\:-x\)
6. \(\:-(-x)\)
7. \(\:\abs{x}\)
8. \(\:-\abs{x}\)
9. \(\:-\abs{-x}\)
10. \(\:x^{-1}\)
\(\underline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}\)
Algebra Refresher Answers
  1. \(\displaystyle \:x \lt 3\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \:x \le 8\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \:x \le -1\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \:x \gt -1\)
  5. Positive
  6. Negative
  7. Positive
  8. Negative
  9. Negative
  10. Negative
