Paragraph

  1. The graph of

    \begin{equation*} \blert{y = A\cos\theta} ~~~\text{or}~~~ \blert{y = A\sin\theta} \end{equation*}
    has amplitude \(\abs{A}\text{.}\)

  2. The graph of

    \begin{equation*} \blert{y =\cos B\theta} ~~~\text{or}~~~ \blert{y = \sin B\theta} \end{equation*}
    has period \(\dfrac{360\degree}{\abs{B}}\text{.}\)

  3. The graph of

    \begin{equation*} \blert{y = k + \cos\theta} ~~~\text{or}~~~ \blert{y =k + \sin\theta} \end{equation*}
    has midline \(y = k\text{.}\)
