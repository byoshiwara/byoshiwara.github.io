71.

\(7\tan \beta - 4 = 2, ~~ 180\degree \lt \beta \lt 270\degree\)

72.

\(3\tan C + 5 = 3, ~~-90\degree \lt C \lt 0\degree\)

73.

\(5\cos \alpha + 3 = 1, ~~ 90\degree \lt \alpha \lt 180\degree\)

74.

\(3\sin \theta + 2 = 4, ~~ 90\degree \lt \beta \lt 180\degree\)
