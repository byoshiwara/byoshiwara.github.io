Because \(\angle G \gt 90\degree\) we know that \(\angle F +\angle G \gt 90\degree +48\degree = 138\degree\text{,}\) so \(\angle F \lt 180\degree-138\degree = 42\degree.\) Thus, \(\angle H \lt \angle F \lt \angle G,\) and consequently \(h \lt f \lt g\text{.}\) We can conclude that \(h \lt 6\) feet long, and \(g \gt 6\) feet long.
