A 5-foot mirror is positioned so that its bottom is 1.5 feet below Jane’s eye level.
  1. Write a formula for \(\alpha\text{,}\) the angle subtended by the section of mirror below Jane’s eye level, when she stands \(x\) feet from the mirror.
  2. Write a formula for \(\theta\text{,}\) the angle subtended by the entire mirror, in terms of \(x\text{.}\)
  3. Evaluate the formula in part (b) for \(x=10\text{,}\) and interpret the result.
