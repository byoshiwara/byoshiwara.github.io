Double Angle Identities.
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] \cos 2\theta \amp = \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta \amp\amp \sin 2\theta = 2\sin \theta \cos \theta\\ \amp = 2\cos^2 \theta -1 \amp\amp \tan 2\theta = \dfrac {2\tan \theta}{1-\tan^2 \theta}\\ \amp = 1-2\sin^2 \theta\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
