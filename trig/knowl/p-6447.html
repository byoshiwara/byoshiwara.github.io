We create a sequence for every point \(c\) in the plane. The collection of all the black points is the Mandelbrot set. It sits in the portion of the plane where \(x\) is between \(-1\) and \(2\text{,}\) and \(y\) is between \(-1.5\) and \(1.5\text{.}\) Outside the set, all the values of \(c\) cause the sequence to go to infinity, and the color of the point is proportional to the speed at which the sequence diverges.
