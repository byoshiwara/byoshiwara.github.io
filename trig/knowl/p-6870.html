We add and subtract complex numbers by combining their real and imaginary parts separately. For example,

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] (4+5i)+(2-3i) \amp = (4+2) + (5-3)i\\ \amp = 6+2i\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
