Because the piston starts at its midline and moves down, we will write \(h(t)\) as a sine function:
\begin{equation*} h(t)=-A\sin (Bt) + k \end{equation*}
The amplitude is \(A=\dfrac{16}{2} = 8\text{,}\) and the midline is \(k=8\text{.}\) The period is \(\dfrac{1}{1000}\text{,}\) so \(\dfrac{B}{1000} = 2\pi\text{,}\) and \(B=2000\pi\text{.}\)
