Exercise 23.
While driving along the interstate, you approach an enormous 50-foot-wide billboard that sits just beside the road. Your viewing angle, \(\theta\text{,}\) increases as you get closer to the billboard.
  1. Write a formula for your distance, \(d\text{,}\) from the billboard as a function of \(\theta\text{.}\)
  2. Write a formula for \(\theta\) as a function of \(d\text{.}\)
  3. Evaluate the formula in part (b) for \(d=200\text{,}\) and interpret the result.
