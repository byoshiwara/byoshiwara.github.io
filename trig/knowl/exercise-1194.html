Exercise 69.

  1. Use your calculator to graph the function \(y = \tan \theta\) in the ZTrig window (press \(\boxed{\text{ZOOM}}~ \boxed{\text{7}}\)), along with the horizontal line \(y = 2\text{.}\) Use the intersect feature to verify that the solutions of the equation \(\tan \theta = 2\) differ by \(180\degree\text{.}\)

  2. Repeat part (a) with the horizontal line \(y = -2\) to verify that the solutions of the equation \(\tan \theta = -2\) differ by \(180\degree\text{.}\)
