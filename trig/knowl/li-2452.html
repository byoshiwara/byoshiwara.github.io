Item b.
Your calculator will give you the values
\begin{align*} \cos (3.5) \amp = -0.9364566873\\ \sin (3.5) \amp = -0.3507832277 \end{align*}
Rounding to four places, we have \(\cos (3.5) = -0.9365\) and \(\sin (3.5) = -0.3508\text{.}\) Note that 3.5 radians is a third-quadrant angle, so the signs of the trig values make sense.
