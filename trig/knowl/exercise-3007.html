Prove the product rule by following the steps.

  1. Suppose \(z_1=a+bi\) and \(z_2=c+di\text{.}\) Compute \(z_1z_2\text{.}\)
  2. Now suppose that \(z_1=r(\cos \alpha +i \sin \alpha)\) and \(z_2=R(\cos \beta +i \sin \beta)\text{.}\) Write \(a,~b,~c\) and \(d\) in terms of \(r,~R,~\alpha\) and \(\beta\text{.}\)
  3. Substitute your expressions for \(a,~b,~c\) and \(d\) into your formula for \(z_1z_2\text{.}\)
  4. Use the laws of sines and cosines to simplify your answer to part (c).
