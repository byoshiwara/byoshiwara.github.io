Answer 77.1.

  1. By the distance formula, \(\sqrt{x^2 + y^2} = r\text{,}\) or \(x^2 + y^2 = r^2\text{.}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{x^2}{r^2} + \dfrac{y^2}{r^2} = 1\)

  3. \(\displaystyle (\dfrac{x}{r})^2 + (\dfrac{y}{r})^2 = 1\)

  4. \(\displaystyle (\cos \theta)^2 + (\sin \theta)^2 = 1\)
