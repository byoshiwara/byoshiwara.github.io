Exercise Group 43–46.

For Problems 43–46,

  1. Graph the function for \(0 \le s \lt 2\pi\text{.}\) State the amplitude, period, and midline of the graph.

  2. Use the graph to solve the equation for \(0 \le s \lt 2\pi\text{.}\)

43.

  1. \(\displaystyle h(s) = 5 + 3\cos 2s\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 5 + 3\cos 2s = 4.56\)

44.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(s) = 4 - 2\sin 3s\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 4 - 2\sin 3s = 2\)

45.

  1. \(\displaystyle g(s) = 10 + 4.8\sin (s - 1.5)\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 10 + 4.8\sin (s - 1.5) = 12\)

46.

  1. \(\displaystyle j(s) = 1.5 + 0.25\cos (s + 0.5)\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 1.5 + 0.25\cos (s + 0.5) = 1.4\)
