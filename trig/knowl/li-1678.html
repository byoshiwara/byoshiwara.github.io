Item 1
Amplitude, Period, and Midline
  1. The graph of
    \begin{equation*} y = A\cos\theta ~~\text{or}~~ y = A\sin\theta \end{equation*}
    has amplitude \(\abs{A}\text{.}\)
  2. The graph of
    \begin{equation*} y =\cos B\theta ~~\text{or}~~ y = \sin B\theta \end{equation*}
    has period \(\dfrac{360\degree}{\abs{B}}\text{.}\)
  3. The graph of
    \begin{equation*} y = k + \cos\theta ~~\text{or}~~ y =k + \sin\theta \end{equation*}
    has midline \(y = k\text{.}\)
