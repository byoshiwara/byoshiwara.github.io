You can use your calculator to graph the sine function, by entering

\begin{equation*} \text{Y}_{1} = \boxed{\text{SIN}}~~ \boxed{X,T,\theta, n}~~\boxed{)} \end{equation*}

and pressing \(\boxed{\text{ZOOM}}~~ \boxed{7}\) for the trig window. The graph shows two periods of the sine function, from \(\theta = -360\degree\) to \(\theta = 360\degree\text{.}\)
