Exercise 42.

Let \(l\) be the line that passes through the origin and the point \((a,b)\text{,}\) where \(a\) and \(b\) are positive numbers. Let \(\theta\) be the angle that \(l\) makes with the positive \(x\)-axis.

  1. Find the value of \(\theta\text{.}\) Hint: Draw the triangle with vertices at the origin, \((a,b)\text{,}\) and \((a,0)\text{,}\) labeling the angle at the origin \(\theta\text{.}\) What is \(\tan \theta\) ?)

  2. What is the slope of the line \(l\) ? How is it related to \(\tan \theta\text{?}\)

  3. Let \(l ^{\prime}\) be any line parallel to \(l\text{.}\) How is the slope of the line \(l ^{\prime}\) related to \(\tan \theta\text{?}\)
