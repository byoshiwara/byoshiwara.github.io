Item a
Keep in mind that \(\arccos \dfrac{1}{3}\) is an angle. We'll call it \(\theta\text{,}\) so that \(\theta = \arccos \dfrac{1}{3}\text{,}\) and hence \(\cos \theta = \dfrac{1}{3}\text{.}\) Then substituting \(\theta\) for \(\arccos \dfrac{1}{3}\) in the original expression gives us
\begin{equation*} \cos\left(\arccos \dfrac{1}{3}\right) = \cos \theta = \dfrac{1}{3} \end{equation*}
