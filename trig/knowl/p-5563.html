From the graph of the secant function, we can see that the equation \(\sec (\theta) =k\) has two solutions between \(0\) and \(2\pi\) if \(k \ge 1\) or \(k \le -1\text{,}\) but no solution for \(-1 \lt k \lt 1\text{.}\) The same is true of the cosecant function: the equation \(\csc (\theta) = k\) has no solution for \(-1 \lt k \lt 1\text{.}\)
