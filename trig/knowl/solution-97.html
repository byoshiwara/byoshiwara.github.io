Solution 5.53.1.

We apply the tangent identity to replace \(~\tan \theta~\) by \(~\dfrac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}~\) and obtain
\begin{align*} \cos \theta \alert{\tan \theta} + \sin \theta \amp = \cos \theta \left(\alert{\dfrac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}}\right) + \sin \theta\\ \amp = \sin \theta + \sin \theta = 2\sin \theta \end{align*}
