Item a.
With your calculator in radian mode, enter \(\boxed{\text{COS}}~(7\pi/4)\) and \(\boxed{\text{SIN}}~ (7\pi/4)\) to find
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] \cos (7\pi/4) \amp = 0.7071067812\\ \sin (7\pi/4) \amp = -0.7071067812\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
Rounding to four decimal places gives \(\cos \dfrac{7\pi}{4} = 0.7071\) and \(\sin \dfrac{7\pi}{4} = -0.7071\text{.}\)
