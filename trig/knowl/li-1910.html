We look for numbers \(p\) and \(q\) so that \((t + p)(t + q) = t^2 - 3t - 10\text{.}\) Their product is \(pq = -10\text{,}\) and their sum is \(p + q = -3\text{.}\) By checking the factors of \(-10\) for the correct sum, we find \(p = -5\) and \(q = 2\text{.}\) Thus,

\begin{equation*} t^2 - 3t - 10 = (t - 5)(t + 2) \end{equation*}
