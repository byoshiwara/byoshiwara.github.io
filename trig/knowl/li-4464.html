Item b

The graph is a cardioid with its axis of symmetry on the \(x\)-axis, and the "bottom" of the heart points in the positive\(x\)direction, so its equation has the form \(r=a+a\cos \theta\text{.}\) At \(\theta=0,~ r=2\text{,}\) so we can solve for \(a\text{:}\) \begin{align*} 2 \amp = a + a\cos 0\\ 2 \amp = a + a(1) =2a\\ \end{align*} Thus, \(a=1\) and we choose the equation \(r=1+\cos \theta\text{.}\)
