Algebra Refresher 10.4.

Write with fractional exponents.
  1. \(\displaystyle \sqrt[3]{a}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{1}{\sqrt[4]{n}}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \sqrt[4]{t^3}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \sqrt[3]{z^5}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x+2}}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \sqrt[3]{(w-1)^2}\)
  7. \(\displaystyle z^3\sqrt[5]{w^4}\)
  8. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{a^2}{\sqrt[4]{b^3}}\)
\(\underline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}\)

  7. \(\displaystyle z^3w^{4/5}\)
  8. \(\displaystyle a^2b^{-3/4}\)
