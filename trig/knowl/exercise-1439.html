An ant walks at constant unit speed (1 unit of distance per second) along the triangle with vertices at \((0,0),~(1,1),\) and \((0,1)\text{.}\)

  1. How far does the ant need to walk to get from \((0,0)\) to \((1,1)\text{?}\) From \((1,1)\) to \((0,1)\text{?}\) From \((0,1)\) to \((0,0)\text{?}\)
  2. Sketch a graph of \(g(t)\text{,}\) the ant's \(y\)-coordinate at time \(t\text{.}\) Show at least two circuits around the triangle.
  3. What is the period of \(g(t)\text{?}\)
