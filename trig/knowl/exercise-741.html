A triangle has base 5 units and altitude 6 units, and another side of length 8 units.

  1. Sketch the triangle so that the acute angle \(\theta\) between the sides of length 5 and 8 is in standard position.
  2. What is the area of the triangle?
  3. What is \(\theta\) ?
  4. Draw another triangle with the same area you found in (b) and with sides 5 and 8, but with an obtuse angle between those two sides. What is the obtuse angle?
