We first compute \({\bf{u}} \cdot {\bf{v}}\) and the magnitude of each vector.

\begin{align*} {\bf{u}} \cdot {\bf{v}} \amp = -3(5)+2(3)=-9\\ \|{\bf{u}}\| \amp = \sqrt{(-3)^2 + 2^2} = \sqrt{13}\\ \|{\bf{v}}\| \amp = \sqrt{5^2 + 3^2} = \sqrt{34}\\ \end{align*}

Then we apply the formula for \(\cos \theta\text{.}\) \begin{align*} \cos \theta \amp = \dfrac{{\bf{v}} \cdot {\bf{w}}}{\|{\bf{v}}\| \|{\bf{w}}\|} = \dfrac{-9}{\sqrt{13}\sqrt{34}} = -0.4281\\ \theta \amp = \cos^{-1}(-0.4281) = 115.35\degree\\ \end{align*} The angle between the vectors is \(115.35\degree\text{.}\)
