Using one of the alternate forms of the Pythagorean identity, we replace \(\cos^2(\theta)\) by \(1 - \sin^2 (\theta)\) to get
\begin{align*} \sin (\theta) \alert{\cos^2 (\theta)} \amp = \sin (\theta) \alert{(1 - \sin^2 (\theta))} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Apply the distributive law.}}\\ \amp = \sin (\theta) - \sin^3 (\theta) \end{align*}
