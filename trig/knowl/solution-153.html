Solution 8.36.1.

  1. We draw a sketch of the triangle formed by the tapestry and the lines of sight to its bottom and top, as shown below. From the triangle we see that \(\tan \theta = \dfrac{3}{d}\text{,}\) so \(d=\dfrac{3}{\tan \theta}\text{.}\)

  2. Because \(\tan \theta = \dfrac{3}{d}\text{,}\) \(\theta = \tan^{-1}\dfrac{3}{d}\text{.}\)
