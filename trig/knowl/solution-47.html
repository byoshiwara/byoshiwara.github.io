Solution 3.38.1

The angle \(\angle ABC = 35\degree + 90\degree = 125\degree\text{.}\) Thus, in \(\triangle ABC\) we have \(a = 34,~ c = 48\) and \(B = 125\degree\text{.}\) The distance from Avery to Clio is represented by \(b\) in the figure.

We know two sides and the included angle, and we choose the version of the Law of Cosines that uses our known angle, \(B\text{.}\) \begin{align*} b^{2} \amp = a^{2} + c^{2} - 2ac \cos B \amp\amp \blert{\text{Substitute the known values.}}\\ b^{2} \amp = 34^{2} + 48^{2} - 2(34)(48) \cos 125\degree \amp\amp \blert{\text{Simplify the right side.}}\\ b^{2} \amp = 3460 - 3264 \cos 125\degree = 5332.153 \amp\amp \blert{\text{Take square roots.}} \\ b \amp = 73.02 \\ \end{align*} Avery is about 73 miles from Clio.
