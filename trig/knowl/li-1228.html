Item A.2.

Draw the perpendicular bisector of side \(\overline{AC}\text{.}\) You can use a compass, or use your ruler and protractor as follows:
  1. Find the midpoint, \(M\text{,}\) of side \(AC\text{.}\)
  2. Draw a line that passes through \(M\) and is perpendicular to side \(\overline{AC}\text{.}\)
