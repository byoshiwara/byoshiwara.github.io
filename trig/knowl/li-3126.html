Item a.
The graph has the shape of \(y=-\cos x\text{.}\) It appears to have midline \(y=75\) and amplitude \(75\text{,}\) so \(k=75\) and \(A=-75\) To find \(B\text{,}\) we compute
\begin{equation*} B=\dfrac{2\pi}{\text{period}}=\dfrac{2\pi}{10.8}=0.58 \end{equation*}
Thus, a formula for the model is \(y=75-75\cos[0.58(x-h)]\text{.}\)
