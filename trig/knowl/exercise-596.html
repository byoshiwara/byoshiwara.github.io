Exercise 47.

Sketch a triangle with angles \(A, ~B\) and \(C\) and opposite sides of lengths respectively \(a,~ b\) and \(c\text{.}\)

  1. Write the area of the triangle in terms of \(a,~b\text{,}\) and angle \(C\text{.}\)

  2. Write the area of the triangle in terms of \(a,~c\text{,}\) and angle \(B\text{.}\)

  3. Write the area of the triangle in terms of \(b,~c\text{,}\) and angle \(A\text{.}\)
