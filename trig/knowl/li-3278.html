Item 1.
Solutions of \(\sin (x) = k,~ k \gt 0\)
  1. Use your calculator to graph \(y=\sin (x)\) and \(y=0.6\) for \(0 \le x \le 2\pi\text{.}\)
  2. Use the Trace and intersect features to find the smallest solution to \(\sin (x) = 0.6\text{,}\) accurate to two decimal places.
  3. Look at the graph, and by symmetry write an expression for the second solution. Hint: What is the \(x\)-coordinate of the first \(x\)-intercept of \(\sin (x)\text{?}\)
