You can verify that both of these numbers are square roots of \(w=-8+8i\sqrt{3}\text{,}\) for instance,

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] \left(2+2i\sqrt{3}\right)^2 \amp = 4+2(2)\left(2i\sqrt{3}\right)+12i^2\\ \amp=-8+8i\sqrt{3}\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
