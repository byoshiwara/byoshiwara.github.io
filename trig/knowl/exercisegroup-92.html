For Problems 65–70,

  1. Sketch an angle \(\theta\) in standard position, \(0\degree \le \theta \le 180\degree\text{,}\) with the given properties.
  2. Find expressions for \(\cos \theta, ~\sin \theta\text{,}\) and \(\tan \theta\) in terms of the given variable.
