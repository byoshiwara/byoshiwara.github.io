Item 1.
  1. Draw an angle \(\theta\) in standard position with the point \(P(6,4)\) on its terminal side.
  2. Find \(r\text{,}\) the distance from the origin to \(P\text{.}\)
  3. Calculate \(\sin (\theta),~ \cos (\theta)\text{,}\) and \(\tan (\theta)\text{.}\) Give both exact answers and decimal approximations rounded to four places.
  4. Use the inverse cosine key on your calculator to find \(\theta\text{.}\) Use your calculator to verify the values of \(\sin (\theta),~ \cos (\theta)\text{,}\) and \(\tan (\theta)\) that you found in part (3).
