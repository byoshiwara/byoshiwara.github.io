Exercise 41.

Let \(l\) be the line that passes through the origin and the point \((4,7)\text{.}\) Let \(\theta\) be the angle that \(l\) makes with the positive \(x\)-axis.

  1. Find the value of \(\theta\text{.}\) Hint: Draw the triangle with vertices at the origin, \((4,7)\text{,}\) and \((4,0)\text{,}\) labeling the angle at the origin \(\theta\text{.}\) What is \(\tan \theta\) ?)

  2. Let \(l ^{\prime}\) be any line parallel to \(l\text{.}\) Find the measure of the acute angle that \(l ^{\prime}\) makes with the positive \(x\)-axis.

  3. What is the slope of the line \(l\) ? How is it related to \(\tan \theta\) ?
