We apply the tangent identity to replace \(\tan \theta\) by \(\dfrac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}\) and obtain \begin{align*} \cos \theta \alert{\tan \theta} + \sin \theta \amp = \cos \theta (\alert{\dfrac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}}) + \sin \theta\\ \amp = \sin \theta + \sin \theta = 2\sin \theta\\ \end{align*}
