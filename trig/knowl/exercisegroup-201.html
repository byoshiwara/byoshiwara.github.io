Exercise Group 5–8.

For Problems 5–8, evaluate the expressions using a calculator. Are they equal?

5.
  1. \(\displaystyle \sin (20\degree + 40\degree)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \sin 20\degree + \sin 40\degree\)
6.
  1. \(\displaystyle \cos^2 70\degree - \sin^2 70\degree\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \cos (2\cdot 70\degree)\)
7.
  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{\sin 55\degree}{\cos 55\degree}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \tan 55\degree\)
8.
  1. \(\displaystyle \tan 80\degree - \tan 10\degree\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \tan (80\degree - 10\degree)\)
