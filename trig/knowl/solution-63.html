Solution 4.27.1

A bearing of \(245\degree\) is in the same direction as an angle of

\begin{equation*} -245\degree + 90\degree = -155\degree \end{equation*}

in standard position, as shown at right, or as the coterminal angle

\begin{equation*} -155\degree + 360\degree = 205\degree\text{.} \end{equation*}

We would like the \(y\)-coordinate of Francine's position, so we calculate \(y = r \sin 205\degree\text{.}\) \begin{align*} y \amp = r \sin 205\degree \\ \amp = 60(-0.4226) = -25.36 \\ \end{align*} Francine is about 25.4 miles south of the airport.
