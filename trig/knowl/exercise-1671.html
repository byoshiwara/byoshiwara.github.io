Exercise 68.

Prove the tangent identity \(\tan t = \dfrac{\sin t}{\cos t}\) by carrying out the following steps. Sketch an arc \(t\) in standard position on a unit circle, and label its terminal point \((x,y)\text{.}\)

  1. Write \(\sin t\) and \(\cos t\) in terms of \(x\) and \(y\text{.}\)

  2. Write the definition of \(\tan t\text{.}\)

  3. Substitute your results from part (a) into your expression for (b).

  4. Does the identity hold for all values of \(t\text{?}\)
