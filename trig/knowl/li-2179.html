Item 5.
Pythagorean Identity.

For any angle \(\theta\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \cos^2 \theta + \sin^2 \theta = 1 \end{equation*}

Alternate forms:

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] \cos^2 \theta \amp = 1 - \sin^2 \theta\\ \sin^2 \theta \amp = 1 - \cos^2 \theta \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
