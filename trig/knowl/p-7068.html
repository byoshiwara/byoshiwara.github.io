Using right triangle trigonometry, we see that

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] a \amp = 3\sqrt{2}\cos \dfrac{\pi}{4} = 3\sqrt{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}} = 3\\ b \amp = 3\sqrt{2} \sin \dfrac{\pi}{4} = 3\sqrt{2} \cdot \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{2}} = 3\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
