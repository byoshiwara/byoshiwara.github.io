Solution 10.56.1.

  1. The conjugate of \(z=7-5i\) is \(\bar{z}=7+5i\text{,}\) so

    \begin{equation*} (z+\bar{z})=(7-5i)+(7+5i) = 14 \end{equation*}
    and
    \begin{align*} z\bar{z} \amp =(7-5i)(7+5i)\\ \amp =49-25i^2 = 49+25=74 \end{align*}

  2. The polynomial is

    \begin{align*} p(x) \amp =x^2 + (z+\bar{z})x+z\bar{z}\\ \amp =x^2-14x+74 \end{align*}
