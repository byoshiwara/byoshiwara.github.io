Exercise 3.

Here are two right triangles with a \(40 \degree\) angle.

  1. Measure the sides \(AB\) and \(AC\) with a ruler. Use the lengths to estimate \(\cos 40\degree\text{.}\)

  2. Measure the sides \(AD\) and \(AE\) with a ruler. Use the lengths to estimate \(\cos 40\degree\text{.}\)

  3. Use your calculator to look up \(\cos 40\degree\text{.}\) Compare your answers. How close were your estimates?
