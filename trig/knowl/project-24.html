Algebra Refresher 8.6.

  1. Solve \(\abs{2x-6}=4\)
  2. Solve \(\abs{1-3x}=10\)
  3. Simplify \(\sqrt{(x-4)^2}\)
  4. Simplify \(\sqrt{(1-x)^2}\)
  5. For what values of \(x\) is \(\abs{x+2}=x+2\) ?
  6. For what values of \(x\) is \(\abs{x-3}=x-3\) ?
  7. Graph the function \(f(x)=\dfrac{x}{\abs{x}}\text{.}\)
  8. Explain the difference between the graphs of \(f(x)=(\sqrt{x})^2\) and \(g(x)=\sqrt{x^2}\text{.}\)
\(\underline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}\)
Algebra Refresher Answers
  1. \(\displaystyle x = 1,~5\)
  2. \(\displaystyle x = -3,~\dfrac{11}{3}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \abs{x-4}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \abs{1-x}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle x\ge -2\)
  6. \(\displaystyle x \lt 3\)
  8. The domain of \(f\) is \([0, \infty)\text{,}\) and the domain of \(g\) is \((-\infty, \infty)\text{.}\)
