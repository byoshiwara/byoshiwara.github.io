  1. Place two stakes at points \(A\) and \(B\text{,}\) 5 units apart.
  2. Draw an arc of radius 4 centered at \(A\text{,}\) and an arc of radius 3 centered at \(B\text{.}\)
  3. Place a third stake at \(C\text{,}\) the intersection of the two arcs.
  4. Because \(3^2 + 4^2 = 5^2\text{,}\) the angle at \(C\) is a right angle.
