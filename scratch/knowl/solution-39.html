Solution A.65.1.

Factor out any common factors from the numerator and denominator. Then divide numerator and denominator by the common factors.

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{8x^3 y}{6x^2 y^3} = \dfrac{4x \cdot 2x^2 y}{3y^2\cdot 2x^2 y}= \dfrac{4x}{3y^2}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{6x-3}{3} = \dfrac{\Ccancel[blue]{3}(2x+1) }{\Ccancel[blue]{3}}= 2x+1 \)
