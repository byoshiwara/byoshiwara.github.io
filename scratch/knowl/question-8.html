Example 1.18. .

Solve \(4x-5=7\)

\begin{align*} \amp \blert{\text{Operations performed on}~x} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Steps for solution}}\\ \amp 1.~~~\text{Multiply by 4} \amp \amp 1.~~~\text{Add 5}\\ \amp 2.~~~\text{Subtract 5} \amp \amp 2.~~~\text{Divide by 4} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} 4x-5\amp =7\amp\amp\blert{\text{Add 5 to both sides.}} \\ 4x-5\blert{+5} \amp =7\blert{+5} \\ 4x \amp =12\amp\amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by }4.} \\ \frac{4x}{\blert{4}}\amp= \frac{12}{\blert{4}} \\ x\amp=3 \end{align*}

The solution is \(3\text{.}\) \(\qquad \blert{\text{Check:}}\quad 4(\alert{3})-5=7 \)
