Activity 1.79. Intercepts and Vertex.
1.

  1. Find the \(x\)-intercepts of

    \begin{equation*} y=2x^2+8x \end{equation*}

  2. Find the vertex of the graph.

  3. Find the \(y\)-intercept of the graph.

  4. Sketch the graph.

2.

  1. Find the \(x\)-intercepts of

    \begin{equation*} y=-x^2-2x +8 \end{equation*}

    (Hint: Factor out \(-1\) from the quadratic trinomial first.)

  2. Find the vertex of the graph.

  3. Find the \(y\)-intercept of the graph.

  4. Sketch the graph.
