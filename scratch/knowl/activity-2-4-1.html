Example 1.18. .

Solve \(4x-5=7\)

\begin{align*} \amp \blert{\text{Operations performed on}~x} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Steps for solution}}\\ \amp 1.~~~\text{Multiply by 4} \amp \amp 1.~~~\text{Add 5}\\ \amp 2.~~~\text{Subtract 5} \amp \amp 2.~~~\text{Divide by 4} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} 4x-5\amp =7\amp\amp\blert{\text{Add 5 to both sides.}} \\ 4x-5\blert{+5} \amp =7\blert{+5} \\ 4x \amp =12\amp\amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by }4.} \\ \frac{4x}{\blert{4}}\amp= \frac{12}{\blert{4}} \\ x\amp=3 \end{align*}

The solution is \(3\text{.}\) \(\qquad \blert{\text{Check:}}\quad 4(\alert{3})-5=7 \)

Example 1.19. .

Solve \(\dfrac{-3t}{4}=6 \)

\begin{align*} \amp \blert{\text{Operations performed on}~t} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Steps for solution}}\\ \amp 1.~~~\text{Multiply by -3} \amp \amp 1.~~~\text{Multiply by 4}\\ \amp 2.~~~\text{Divide by 4} \amp \amp 2.~~~\text{Divide by -3} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} \frac{-3t}{4}\amp =6\amp\amp\blert{\text{Multiply both sides by 4.}} \\ \blert{4}\left(\frac{-3t}{4} \right)\amp =(6)\blert{4} \\ -3t \amp =24\amp\amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by }-3.} \\ \frac{-3t}{\blert{-3}}\amp= \frac{24}{\blert{-3}} \\ t\amp=-8 \end{align*}

The solution is \(-8\text{.}\) \(\qquad \blert{\text{Check:}}\quad \dfrac{-3(\alert{-8})}{4}=6 \)

Example 1.20. .

Solve \(5(z-6)=65 \)

\begin{align*} \amp \blert{\text{Operations performed on}~z} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Steps for solution}}\\ \amp 1.~~~\text{Subtract 6} \amp \amp 1.~~~\text{Divide by 5}\\ \amp 2.~~~\text{Multiply by 5} \amp \amp 2.~~~\text{Add 6} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} 5(z-6) \amp =65\amp\amp\blert{\text{Divide both sides by 5.}} \\ \frac{5(z-6)}{\blert{5}}\amp= \frac{65}{\blert{5}}\\ z-6 \amp =13\amp\amp \blert{\text{Add 6 to both sides.}} \\ z-6 \blert{+6}\amp= 13 \blert{+6} \\ z\amp=19 \end{align*}

The solution is \(19\text{.}\) \(\qquad \blert{\text{Check:}}\quad 5(\alert{19}-6)=65 \)
