Choose scales for the axes: Use 10 grid lines on the horizontal axis. The length of each interval is

\begin{equation*} 20\div 10=\underline{\qquad\qquad\qquad}\text{ units} \end{equation*}

so we can scale the horizontal axis in intervals of 2. On the vertical axis, we use increments of 25 from 0 to 400, which gives us 16 grid lines.
