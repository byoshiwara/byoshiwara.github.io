Activity 1.26. Problem Solving.
1.

Mitch bought a Blu-Ray player for $269 and a number of discs at $14 each.

  1. Write an equation for Mitch's total bill, \(B\text{,}\) in terms of the number of discs, \(d\text{,}\) he bought.

  2. If the total bill before tax was $367, how many discs did Mitch buy?

    \(\blert{\text{Substitute 367 for } B \text{ and solve for } d.} \)

    \(\blert{\text{Write your answer in a sentence.}} \)

2.

Home Station had a promotion offering $4 off on a gallon of house paint.

  1. Write an equation for the cost, \(C\text{,}\) of 15 gallons of paint in terms of the regular price, \(p\text{.}\)

  2. Randall bought 15 gallons and paid $480 before tax. What is the regular price of a gallon of paint.
