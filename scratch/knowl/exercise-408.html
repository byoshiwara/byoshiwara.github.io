Worksheet Exercise 1.

Milly's Muffins can sell \(x\) muffins at the farmers' market if she charges \(6-0.02x\) dollars for a muffin. It costs Milly \(50+1.5x\) dollars to make \(x\) muffins.

  1. Write a polynomial for Milly's revenue from selling muffins.

    Hint: \(\text{Revenue}=(\text{number of items sole}) \times (\text{price per item}) \)

  2. Write a polynomial for Milly's profit.

  3. Find Milly's profit if she sells 50 muffins, 100 muffins, and 150 muffins.
