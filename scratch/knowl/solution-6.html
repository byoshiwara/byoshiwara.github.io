Solution A.11.1.

We begin by isolating the term that contains \(C\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} 5F \amp= 9C + 160 \amp\amp\blert{\text{Subtract 160 from both sides.}}\\ 5F - 160 \amp = 9C \amp\amp\blert{\text{Divide both sides by 9.}}\\ \frac{5F - 160}{9} \amp =C \end{align*}

We can also write the formula for \(C\) in terms of \(F\) as \(~C = \dfrac{5}{9}F - \dfrac{160}{9}\text{.}\)
