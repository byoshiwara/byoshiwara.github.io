Step 2

The product of the \(\blert{\text{L}}\)ast terms \(18\text{,}\) so \(pq=18\text{.}\) There are three possibilities for \(p\) and \(q\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} 1~ \text{ and } ~18, \qquad 2~ \text{ and } ~9, \qquad \text{ or } \qquad 3~ \text{ and } ~6 \end{equation*}

We use the middle terms of the trinomial to decide which possibility is correct.
