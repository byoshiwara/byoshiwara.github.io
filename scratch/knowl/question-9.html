Solve \(\dfrac{-3t}{4}=6 \)

\begin{align*} \amp \blert{\text{Operations performed on}~t} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Steps for solution}}\\ \amp 1.~~~\text{Multiply by -3} \amp \amp 1.~~~\text{Multiply by 4}\\ \amp 2.~~~\text{Divide by 4} \amp \amp 2.~~~\text{Divide by -3} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} \frac{-3t}{4}\amp =6\amp\amp\blert{\text{Multiply both sides by 4.}} \\ \blert{4}\left(\frac{-3t}{4} \right)\amp =(6)\blert{4} \\ -3t \amp =24\amp\amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by }-3.} \\ \frac{-3t}{\blert{-3}}\amp= \frac{24}{\blert{-3}} \\ t\amp=-8 \end{align*}

The solution is \(-8\text{.}\) \(\qquad \blert{\text{Check:}}\quad \dfrac{-3(\alert{-8})}{4}=6 \)
