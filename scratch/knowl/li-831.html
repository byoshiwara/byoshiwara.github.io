Step 2

There are only two ways to factor 15, either 1 times 15 or 3 times 5. We just guess that the second factor is negative, and check \(\blert{O+I} \) for each possibility:

\begin{align*} \amp(x+1)(x-15)\amp\amp \color{blue}{O+I=-15x+x=-14x }\\ \amp(x+3)(x-5)\amp\amp \color{blue}{O+I=-5x+3x=-\boldsymbol{2x}} \end{align*}
