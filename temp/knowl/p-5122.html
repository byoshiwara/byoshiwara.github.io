\begin{equation*} x = y^2 - 4 \end{equation*}

and solve for \(y\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] y^2 \amp = x-4\\ y \amp = \pm \sqrt{x-4}\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
