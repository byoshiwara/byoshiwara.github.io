Exercise 1.

Here are two right triangles with a \(65 \degree\) angle.

  1. Measure the sides \(AB\) and \(BC\) with a ruler. Use the lengths to estimate \(\sin 65\degree\text{.}\)
  2. Measure the sides \(AD\) and \(DE\) with a ruler. Use the lengths to estimate \(\sin 65\degree\text{.}\)
  3. Use your calculator to look up \(\sin 65\degree\text{.}\) Compare your answers. How close were your estimates?
