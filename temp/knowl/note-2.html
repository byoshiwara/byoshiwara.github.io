<<<<<<< HEAD Note 1.28.

The relationship between an equation and its graph is an important one. For the previous example, make sure you understand that the following three statements are equivalent.

  1. The point \((9, 150)\) lies on the graph of \(y = 285 - 15x\text{.}\)

  2. The ordered pair \((9, 150)\) is a solution of the equation \(y = 285 - 15x\text{.}\)

  3. \(x=9\) is a solution of the equation \(150 = 285 - 15x\text{.}\)
