in-context =======

A polynomial has a zero "of multiplicity \(k\)" at \(x=a\) if \((x-a)\) occurs \(k\) times as a factor of the polynomial. For example, the polynomial \(p(x) = (x-5)^2\) has a zero of multiplicity 2 at \(x=5\text{.}\)
