\begin{align*} x^2+5 \amp = 0 \amp \amp \blert{\text{Solve by extraction of roots.}}\\ x^2 \amp = -5\\ x \amp = \pm \sqrt{-5} \end{align*}

Because the square root of a negative number is undefined, this parabola has no \(x\)-intercepts.
