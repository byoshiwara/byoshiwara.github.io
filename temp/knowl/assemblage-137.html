Ratio.

A ratio is a type of quotient used to compare two numerical quantities. The ratio of \(a\) to \(b\) is written \(\dfrac{a}{b}\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \text{Range: } \dfrac{-\pi}{2} \le y \le \dfrac{\pi}{2} \end{equation*}
