Point-Slope Formula.

To find an equation for the line of slope \(m\) passing through the point \((x_1,y_1)\text{,}\) we use the point-slope formula

\begin{equation*} \blert{\dfrac{y-y_1}{x-x_1} = m} \end{equation*}

or

\begin{equation*} \blert{y-y_1 = m(x-x_1)} \end{equation*}

Quotient of Complex Numbers.

The quotient of two complex numbers \(z_1=a+bi\) and \(z_2=c+di\) is

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{z_1}{z_2} = \dfrac{a+bi}{c+di} = \dfrac{a+bi}{c+di} \cdot \dfrac{c-di}{c-di} = \dfrac{ac+bd}{c^2+d^2} + \dfrac{bc-ad}{c^2+d^2}i \end{equation*}
