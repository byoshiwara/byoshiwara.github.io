Two-Point Formula for Slope.

The slope of the line joining points \(P_1(x_1,y_1)\) and \(P_2(x_2,y_2)\) is

\begin{equation*} \blert{m=\dfrac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}}~~~~~~\text{if}~~~~x_2 \not= x_1 \end{equation*}
