Some examples of quadratic equations are

\begin{equation*} 2x^2+5x-3=0,~~~~7t-t^2=0,~~~~\text{and}~~~~3w^2=16 \end{equation*}

The first equation is already written in standard form, with \(a=2,~b=5\) and \(c=-3\text{.}\) In standard form, the other two equations are

\begin{align*} -t^2+7t+0 \amp =0 \amp \amp \text{so} ~ a=-1,~b=7,~ \text{and}~ c=0\\ 3w^2+0w-16 \amp =0 \amp \amp \text{so} ~ a=3,~b=0,~ \text{and}~ c=-16 \end{align*}
