\(x\) Calculation \(y\) \((x,y)\)
\(0\) \(y=8-0=8\) \(8\) \((0,8)\)
\(2\) \(y=8-2=6\) \(6\) \((2,6)\)
\(5\) \(y=8-5=3\) \(3\) \((5,3)\)
\(8\) \(y=8-8=0\) \(0\) \((8,0)\)
