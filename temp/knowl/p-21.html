What is the length of the radius of the inscribed circle?

  1. Write the area of \(\Delta ABC\) as the sum of the areas of the six triangles in terms of \(r\text{,}\) \(x\text{,}\) \(y\text{,}\) and \(z\text{.}\)

    Combine like terms, then factor out the greatest common factor of the terms.

  2. Write the perimeter of \(\Delta ABC\) in terms of \(x\text{,}\) \(y\text{,}\) and \(z\text{.}\) (Can you see why the sum of \(x\text{,}\) \(y\text{,}\) and \(z\) is called the semiperimeter?)

  3. Show that the radius of the inscribed circle is the area of the triangle divided by its semiperimeter.
