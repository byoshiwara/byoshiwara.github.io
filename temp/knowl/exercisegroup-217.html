Exercise Group 8–9.

Solve Problems 8–9 about motion.

8.

Delbert and Francine leave Cedar Rapids at the same time and drive in opposite directions for 6 hours.

  1. Choose variables for Delbert and Francine's speeds and fill in the table.

    \(\hphantom{0000}\) Rate Time Distance
    Delbert \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\)
    Francine \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\)

  2. Make a sketch showing Cedar Rapids, Delbert, and Francine. Label your sketch with the distance that each traveled.
  3. Francine drove 5 miles per hour slower than Delbert. After 6 hours, they are 570 miles apart. Write two equations about the problem.
  4. Solve your system to find Delbert's and Francine's speeds.
