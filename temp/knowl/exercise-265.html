Exercise 16.

There were 42 passengers on a local airplane flight. First-class fare was $80, and coach fare was $64. If the revenue for the flight totaled $2880, how many first-class and how many coach passengers paid for the flight?

  1. Write algebraic expressions to fill in the table.

    Number of tickets Cost per ticket Revenue
    First-class \(x\)
    Coach \(y\)
    Total

  2. Write an equation about the number of tickets sold.

  3. Write a second equation about the revenue from the tickets.

  4. Graph both equations on graph paper and solve the system. (Hint: Find the intercepts of each equation to help you choose scales for the axes.)

  5. Answer the question in the problem.
