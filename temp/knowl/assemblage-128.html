Sum and Difference Identities for Tangent.
\begin{equation*} \tan (\alpha + \beta) = \dfrac{\tan \alpha + \tan \beta}{1 - \tan \alpha \tan \beta} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \tan (\alpha - \beta) = \dfrac{\tan \alpha - \tan \beta}{1 + \tan \alpha \tan \beta} \end{equation*}
