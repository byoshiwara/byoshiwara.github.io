Proportional Variables.

Two variables are proportional if their ratio is always the same.

in-context =======
\begin{equation*} y = \tan^{-1}x \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{Domain: } -\infty \lt x \lt \infty \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{Range: } \dfrac{-\pi}{2} \lt y \lt \dfrac{\pi}{2} \end{equation*}
