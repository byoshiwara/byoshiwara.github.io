\begin{align*} 2x+3y~~~~~~~~ \amp \text{cannot be simplified}\\ -6x+4xy~~~~~~~~ \amp \text{cannot be simplified}\\ 6-2x~~~~~~~~ \amp \text{cannot be simplified} \end{align*}
in-context ======= Caution 4.18.

When you ask your calculator to find an angle with a given sine by pressing SIN, it will give you only one of the many possible answers. The same is true of the COS and TAN keys. You must be careful to choose the appropriate answer for the situation, using reference and coterminal angles.
