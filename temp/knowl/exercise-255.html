Exercise 1.

Choose the correct algebraic expression for each phrase.

  1. 6 times the sum of \(x\) and 5

    \begin{equation*} 6x+5~~~~~~~~\text{or}~~~~~~~~6(x+5) \end{equation*}

  2. \dfrac{1}{2} the difference of \(p\) and \(q\)

    \begin{equation*} \dfrac{1}{2}(p-q)~~~~~~~~\text{or}~~~~~~~~\dfrac{1}{2}p-q \end{equation*}

  3. 4 less than the product of 6 and \(w\)

    \begin{equation*} 6w-4~~~~~~~~\text{or}~~~~~~~~4-6w \end{equation*}

  4. 2 less than the product of 10 and \(z\)

    \begin{equation*} \dfrac{10}{z}-2~~~~~~~~\text{or}~~~~~~~~2-\dfrac{10}{z} \end{equation*}
