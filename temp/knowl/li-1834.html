in-context ======= Item 23.
Periodic Function.

The function \(y = f(x)\) is periodic if there is a smallest value of \(p\) such that

\begin{equation*} f(x + p) = f(x) \end{equation*}

for all \(x\text{.}\) The constant \(p\) is called the period of the function.
