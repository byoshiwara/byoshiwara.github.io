More Inverse Operations.

Addition and subtraction are opposite or inverse operations, because each operation undoes the effects of the other.

in-context =======
Trigonometric Ratios.

If \(\theta\) is one of the angles in a right triangle,

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] \sin \theta \amp = \dfrac{\text{opposite}}{\text{hypotenuse}} \\ \cos \theta \amp = \dfrac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}} \\ \tan \theta \amp = \dfrac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}} \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
