in-context ======= Item 12.
Definitions of the inverse trig functions.
\begin{equation*} \sin^{-1}x=\theta~~~\text{if and only if}~~~~\sin \theta = x~~~\text{and}~~~\dfrac{-\pi}{2} \le \theta \le \dfrac{\pi}{2} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \cos^{-1}x=\theta~~~\text{if and only if}~~~~\cos \theta = x~~~\text{and}~~~0 \le \theta \le \pi \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \tan^{-1}x=\theta~~~\text{if and only if}~~~~\tan \theta = x~~~\text{and}~~~\dfrac{-\pi}{2} \lt \theta \lt \dfrac{\pi}{2} \end{equation*}
