Subtracting a positive number.

Illustrate the sum \(9+(-5)\) on a number line.

  1. Plot \(9\) on the number line.
  2. Move \(5\) units in the negative direction, or to the left.
  3. The sum is \(4\text{.}\)

Periodic Function.

The function \(y = f(x)\) is periodic if there is a smallest value of \(p\) such that

\begin{equation*} f(x + p) = f(x) \end{equation*}

for all \(x\text{.}\) The constant \(p\) is called the period of the function.
