Call for new officers

Sam Nelson, Past Section Chair, Claremont McKenna College, Claremont, CA

The SoCal-Nevada Section of the Mathematical Association of America is looking for new officers! Immediately available is the position of Assistant Meetings Coordinator. Positions to be elected next spring include MAA Congress Representative (to the national MAA), 2nd Vice Program Chair, Section Vice Chair, Secretary, and Treasurer.

Serving as an officer looks great on your CV and gives you a chance to help shape mathematics in SoCal and Nevada. The officers organize the fall and spring sectional MAA conferences, select the annual Teaching Award winner and have opportunities to attend MathFest and otherwise get involved in the national MAA organization. Let us know if you are interested!

News of the Section

The section is pleased to wecome several new officers!
Emily Cilli-Turner of the University of La Verne has been appointed as the Section NExT liaison. Mary Margarita Legner of the Riverside City College has been selected to be the Vice Program Chair. Zhijian Wu of the University of Nevada Las Vegas has been appointed as the Nevada liaison of the MAA Southern California-Nevada Section. Shanna Dobson of UCLA, CSULA, and Art Center College of Design has been appointed as Newsletter editor.

Pomona College mathematics professor Stephan Ramon Garcia has been named one of the 2019 Class of the Fellows of the American Mathematical Society and as winner of the first AMS Mary P. Dolciani Prize for Excellence in Research. He is cited for his outstanding record of research in operator theory, complex analysis, matrix theory, and number theory, for high quality scholarship with a diverse set of undergraduates, and for his service to the profession.
