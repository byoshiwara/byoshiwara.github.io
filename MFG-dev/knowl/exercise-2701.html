Exercise 10.
The data show sales of in-line skates at a sporting goods store at the beach.
Year \(1990\) \(1991\) \(1992\) \(1993\) \(1994\)
Skate sold \(54\) \(82\) \(194\) \(446\) \(726\)
  1. Use the values for \(1991\) through \(1993\) to fit a quadratic function to the data, \(S = at^2 + bt + c\text{,}\) where \(t\) is measured in years since \(1990\text{.}\)
  2. What does your function predict for the number of pairs of skates sold in \(1994\text{?}\)
  3. Sketch the graph of your function and the given data.
