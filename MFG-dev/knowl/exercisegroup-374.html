Exercise Group 37–42.

37.

  1. \(\displaystyle z=3i\)

  2. \(\displaystyle z=-3i\)

38.

\(2y^2 - y - 2\)

  1. \(\displaystyle y=2-i\)

  2. \(\displaystyle y=-2-i\)

39.

\(x^2 - 2x + 2\)

  1. \(\displaystyle x=1-i\)

  2. \(\displaystyle x=1+i\)

40.

\(3w^2 +5\)

  1. \(\displaystyle w=2i\)

  2. \(\displaystyle w=-2i\)

41.

\(q^2 +4q + 13\)

  1. \(\displaystyle q = -2 + 3i\)

  2. \(\displaystyle q = -2 - 3i\)

42.

\(v^2 + 2v + 3\)

  2. \(\displaystyle v=-1 + i\)
