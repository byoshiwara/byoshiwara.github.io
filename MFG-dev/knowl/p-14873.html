This triangular matrix corresponds to the system

\begin{alignat*}{5} -w\amp {}+{} \amp x \amp {}-{}\amp y\amp {}+{}\amp z \amp =\amp 7\\ \amp {} {}\amp 6x\amp {}+{}\amp 6y\amp {}+{}\amp 3z \amp =\amp 9\\ \amp {}{}\amp \amp {} {}\amp 6y\amp {}+{}\amp 3z\amp =\amp -3\\ \amp {} {}\amp \amp {} {}\amp \amp {} {}\amp 12z\amp =\amp 36 \end{alignat*}

which we solve by back-substitution. From the last equation, we find \(z=3\text{,}\) and by substituting \(3\) for \(z\) in the third equation, we find \(y=-1\text{.}\) Next, we substitute \(-1\) for \(y\) and \(3\) for \(z\) in the second equation to find \(x=1\text{,}\) and finally, we substitute \(x=1\text{,}\) \(y=-1\text{,}\) \(z=3\) into the first equation to find \(w=-2\text{.}\)
