Exercise 63.

The sail in the figure is a right triangle of base and height \(x\text{.}\) It has a colored stripe along the hypotenuse and a white triangle of base and height \(y\) in the lower corner.

  1. Write an expression for the area of the colored stripe.

  2. Express the area of the stripe in factored form.

  3. If the sail is \(7\frac{1}{2} \) feet high and the white strip is \(4\frac{1}{2} \) feet high, use your answer to (b) to calculate mentally the area of the stripe.
