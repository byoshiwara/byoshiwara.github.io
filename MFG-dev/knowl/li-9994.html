Substitute \(170\) for \(V\) and solve for \(r\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} 170 \amp = 6\pi r^2\amp\amp\text{Divide both sides by }6\pi.\\ r^2 \amp = \frac{170}{6\pi} \amp\amp\text{Take square roots.}\\ r \amp = \sqrt{\frac{170}{6\pi}}\approx 3.00312 \end{align*}
