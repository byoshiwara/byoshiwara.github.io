Laws of Exponents.

  1. \(\displaystyle a^m\cdot a^n = a^{m+n}\)

    1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{a^m}{a^n}=a^{m-n} \hphantom{blank}m\gt n\)

    2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle{\frac{a^m}{a^n}=\frac{1}{a^{n-m}} \hphantom{blank}m\lt n}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \left(a^m\right)^n=a^{m+n}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle (ab)^n=a^n b^n\)

  5. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle{\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n} }\)
