Item 1.30.c.

What do the intercepts tell us about the problem?

The \(x\)-intercept tells us:

  • the total water usage of corn.

  • the cost of the corn if no wheat is planted.

  • the optimal number of acres of corn.

  • the total acres if all corn is planted.

The \(y\)-intercept tells us:

  • the largest possible number of acres of wheat.

  • the amount of water used by the wheat.

  • the total cost of the wheat.

  • how many acres are left after the wheat is planted.
