Project 9.8.

In this project we will find a formula for the sum of the arithmetic series

\begin{equation*} 1+2+3+\cdots+(N-2)+(N-1)+N \end{equation*}

This is the sum of the first \(N\) positive integers.

  1. How many terms are in the series?

  2. If we call this sum \(S\text{,}\) then

    \begin{equation*} S=1+2+3+\cdots+(N-2)+(N-1)+N \end{equation*}

    Rewrite the sum by reversing the order of the terms on the right side of the equation and add the result "columnwise" to the equation above. What is the sum of the two numbers in any one column on the right side?

  3. From part (a) you know how many columns there are. Based on that number and your answer to part (b), write an expression for \(2S\) and solve for \(S\text{.}\)

  4. Use your answer from part (c) to write a formula for the sum of the arithmetic series

    \begin{equation*} 1+2+3+\cdots+(N-2)+(N-1)+N \end{equation*}
