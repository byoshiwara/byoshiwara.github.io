Item a

To evaluate \(\log_3 9\text{,}\) we ask what exponent on base \(3\) will produce \(9\text{.}\) In symbols, we want to fill in the blank in the equation \(3^{\underline{?}} = 9\text{.}\) The exponent we need is \(2\text{,}\) so

\begin{equation*} \log_3 9 = 2 \text{ because } 3^2 = 9 \end{equation*}

We use similar reasoning to compute the other logarithms.
