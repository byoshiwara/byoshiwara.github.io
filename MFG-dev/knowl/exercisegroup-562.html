For Problems 33 and 33, recall from geometry that the altitude from one vertex of a triangle to the opposite side is perpendicular to that side.

33.

  1. Sketch the triangle with vertices \(A(-6,-3),~B(-6,3)\) and \(C(4,5)\text{.}\)

  2. Find the slope of the side \(\overline{AC}\text{.}\)

  3. Find the slope of the altitude from point \(B\) to side \(\overline{BC}\text{.}\)

  4. Find an equation for the line that includes the altitude from point \(B\) to side \(\overline{BC}\text{.}\)
