In New York City, taxi cabs charge $2.50 for distances up to \(\dfrac{1}{3} \) mile, plus $0.40 for each additional \(\dfrac{1}{5} \) mile or portion thereof. (Source: www.visitnyc.com)

  1. Sketch a graph of \(F(d)\text{,}\) which gives taxi fare as a function of distance traveled, on the domain \(0\lt d \lt 1\text{.}\)

  2. State the range of \(F(d)\) on that domain.

  3. How much will it cost Renee to travel by taxi from Columbia University to Rockefeller Center, a distance of 5.7 miles?
