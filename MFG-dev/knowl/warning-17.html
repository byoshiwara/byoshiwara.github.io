Caution 4.78

Knowing only two points on the graph of f is not enough to tell us what kind of function \(f\) is. Through the two points in Example 4.77, we can also fit a linear function or a power function. You can check that the three functions below all satisfy \(f (2) = 4.5\) and \(f (5) = 121.5\text{.}\) The graphs of the functions are shown in Figure 4.79.

\begin{align*} L(x) \amp = -73.5 + 39x \\ P(x) \amp = 0.372x^{3.6} \\ E(x) \amp = 0.5(3^x ) \\ \end{align*}
