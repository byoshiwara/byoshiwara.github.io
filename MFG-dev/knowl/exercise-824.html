Exercise 49

  1. Write the piecewise definition for \(\abs{3x - 6}\text{.}\)

  2. Use your answer to part (a) to write two inequalities that together are equivalent to \(\abs{3x - 6}\lt 9\text{.}\)

  3. Solve the inequalities in part (b) and check that the solutions agree with the solutions of \(\abs{3x - 6}\lt 9\text{.}\)

  4. Show that \(\abs{3x - 6}\lt 9\) is equivalent to the compound inequality \(-9\lt 3x - 6\lt 9\text{.}\)
