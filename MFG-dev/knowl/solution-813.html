Solution A.29.1.

  1. The point with first coordinate \(15,000\) on the graph at right has second coordinate approximately \(17.4\text{.}\) We estimate the pressure at \(15,000\) feet to be \(17.4\) inches of mercury.

  2. The point on the graph with second coordinate \(12\) has first coordinate approximately \(25,000\text{,}\) so an atmospheric pressure of \(12\) inches of mercury occurs at about \(25,000\) feet.
