Exercise 12.

The scatterplot shows the outside temperature and the number of cups of cocoa sold at an outdoor skating rink snack bar on 13 consecutive nights.

  1. How many cups of cocoa were sold when the temperature was \(2\degree\)C?

  2. What was the temperature on the night when \(25\) cups of cocoa were sold?

  3. Use a straightedge to draw a line of best fit through the data points

  4. Use your line of best fit to predict the number of cups of cocoa that will be sold at the snack bar if the temperature is \(7\degree\)C.

  5. Choose two points on your regression line and find its equation.

  6. Use the equation to predict the number of cups of cocoa that will be sold when the temperature is \(10\degree\)C and when the temperature is \(24\degree\)C. Are these predictions reasonable?
