Exercise Group 53–56.

In Problems 53–56, choose the correct graph for each equation. The scales on both axes are the same.
53.
  1. \(\displaystyle y = \dfrac{3}{4}x + 2\)
  2. \(\displaystyle y = \dfrac{-3}{4}x + 2\)
  3. \(\displaystyle y = \dfrac{3}{4}x - 2 \)
  4. \(\displaystyle y =\dfrac{-3}{4}x - 2\)
54.

  2. \(m\gt 1\text{,}\) \(b\lt 0\)
  3. \(0\lt m\lt 1\text{,}\) \(b\lt 0\)
  4. \(m\lt -1\text{,}\) \(b\lt 0 \)

  1. \(\displaystyle y = 1 + 2(x+3) \)
  2. \(\displaystyle y = -1 + 2(x-3)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle y = -1 + 2(x+3) \)
  4. \(\displaystyle y = 1 + 2(x-3) \)
