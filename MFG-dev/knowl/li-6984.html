Item 50.a

If there were no air resistance, the height, \(x\text{,}\) of the ball t seconds after its release would be given in meters by the formula

\begin{equation*} h=\frac{vt}{\sqrt{2}}-\frac{gt^2}{2} \end{equation*}

where \(g\) is the acceleration due to gravity. Find an expression for the total time the ball is in the air. (Hint: Set \(h=0\) and solve for \(t\) in terms of the other variables.)
