What is the horizontal asymptote of the graph?

Equation of asymptote: Use \(x\) for the independent variable, and use \(C\) for the dependent variable.

What does the horizontal asymptote tell you about the solution?

  • Delbert cannot add more than 100 mL glucose to the mixture.

  • If Delbert adds 1 mL of glucose to the mixture, its concentration becomes infinite.

  • As Delbert adds more glucose to the mixture, its concentration increases toward 100%.
