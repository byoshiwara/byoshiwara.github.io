Solution 5.81.1

  1. The growth factor was \(b = 1 + r = 1.064\text{.}\) The population \(t\) years later was

    \begin{equation*} P(t) = 768,000 (1.064)^t \end{equation*}

  2. We use the formula \(P(t) = P_0 \cdot e^{kt}\text{,}\) where \(e^k = 1.064\text{.}\) Solving for \(k\text{,}\) we find

    \begin{equation*} k = \ln 1.064 = 0.062 \end{equation*}

    so \(P(t) = 768,000 e^{0.062t}\text{.}\)
