If you would like to graph the regression equation on top of the scatterplot, first follow the steps in Making a Scatterplot and Finding a Regression Equation above.

  1. Press Y= and clear out any old equations.

  2. Position the cursor after \(Y_1 =\text{.}\)

  3. Press VARS \(5\) → → ENTER to copy the regression equation.

  4. Press GRAPH to see graph of the regression equation and the scatterplot.
