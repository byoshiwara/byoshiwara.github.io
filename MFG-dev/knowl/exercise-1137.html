Exercise 44.

The weight, \(w\text{,}\) of a bronze statue varies directly with the cube of its height, \(h\text{.}\)

  1. If you double the height of the statue, what happens to its weight?

  2. If you make the statue one-fourth as tall, what happens to its weight?

  3. If you increase the height of the statue by \(50\%\text{,}\) what happens to its weight?
