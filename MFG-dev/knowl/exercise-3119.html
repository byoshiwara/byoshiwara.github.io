Exercise 42.

On New Year's Day, a blimp leaves its berth in Carson, California, and heads north for the Rose Bowl, \(23\) miles away. There is a breeze from the north at \(6\) miles per hour.

  1. Express the time required for the trip as a function of the blimp's airspeed.

  2. Write a function for the time needed for the return trip.

  3. Which trip takes longer? By how much time (in terms of the blimp's airspeed)?
