Item 5.78.c.

Write and solve an equation to answer the question: How long does it take for 60% of the krypton-91 to decay?

  • \(25e^{-0.07t}= 0.60 \)

  • \(25e^{-0.07t}= 0.60 (25) \)

  • \(25e^{-0.07t}= 0.40 (25) \)

  • None of the above
