A small company manufactures radios. When it charges $\(20\) for a radio, it sells \(500\) radios per month. For each dollar the price is increased, \(10\) fewer radios are sold per month.
  1. Write a function for the monthly revenue in terms of the price increase over $\(20\text{.}\)
  2. What should the company charge for a radio in order to maximize its monthly revenue?
