A mobile home loses \(20\%\) of its value every \(3\) years.
  1. A certain mobile home costs \(\$20,000\text{.}\) Write a function for its value after \(t\) years.
  2. Use your calculator to graph your function on the interval \(0 \le t\le 30\text{.}\)
  3. How long will it be before a \(\$20,000\) mobile home depreciates to \(\$12,800\text{?}\) Use algebraic methods to find your answer, and verify it on your graph.
