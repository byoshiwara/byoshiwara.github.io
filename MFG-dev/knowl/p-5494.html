Leslie plans to invest some money in two CD accounts. The first account pays \(3.6\%\) interest per year, and the second account pays \(2.8\%\) interest per year. Leslie would like to earn $\(500\) per year on her investment.
  1. Write a linear equation in general form that relates \(x\text{,}\) the amount Leslie invests at \(3.4\%\text{,}\) and \(y\text{,}\) the amount she invests at \(2.8\%\text{.}\)
  2. Use your equation from part (a) to write \(y\) as a function of \(x\text{,}\) \(y = f (x)\text{.}\)
  3. Find the domain and range of \(f\text{.}\)
