Exercise16

Kate and Julie set out in their sailboat on a straight course at 9 miles per hour. Two hours later, their mother becomes worried and sends their father after them in the speedboat. If their father travels at 24 miles per hour, how long will it be before he catches them?

  1. What are we asked to find in this problem? Assign a variable to represent it.

  2. Write an expression in terms of your variable for the distance Kate and Julie sailed.

  3. Write an expression in terms of your variable for the distance their father traveled.

  4. Write an equation and solve it.
