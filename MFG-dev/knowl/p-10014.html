The population of the state of California increased during the years 1990 to 2000 according to the formula
\begin{equation*} P(t) = 29,760,021 (10)^{0.0056t} \end{equation*}
where \(t\) is measured in years since \(1990\text{.}\)
  1. What was the population in \(2000\text{?}\)
  2. Assuming the same rate of growth, estimate the population of California in the years \(2010\text{,}\) \(2015\text{,}\) and \(2020\text{.}\)
  3. When did the population of California reach \(35,000,000\text{?}\)
  4. When should the population reach \(40,000,000\text{?}\)
  5. Graph the function \(P\) with a suitable domain and range, then verify your answers to parts (a) through (d).
