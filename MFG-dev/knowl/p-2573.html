For Problems 43 and 44, the equation of line \(l_1\) is \(y = q + px\text{,}\) and the equation of line \(l_2\) is \(y = v + tx\text{.}\)
  1. Decide whether the coordinates of each labeled point are
    1. a solution of \(y = q + px\text{,}\)
    2. a solution of \(y = v + tx\text{,}\)
    3. a solution of both equations, or
    4. a solution of neither equation.
  2. Find \(p\text{,}\) \(q\text{,}\) \(t\text{,}\) and \(v\text{.}\)
