Item d.

In a study of ladybirds, it was discovered that larvae in their second stage of development consumed aphids at a rate \(y_2\) aphids per day, given by

\begin{equation*} y_2=\frac{20x}{x+16} \end{equation*}

where \(x\) is the number of aphids available. Larvae in the third stage ate at rate \(y_3\text{,}\) given by

\begin{equation*} y_3=\frac{90x}{x+79} \end{equation*}

Graph both of these functions on the domain \(0\le x\le 140\text{.}\)
