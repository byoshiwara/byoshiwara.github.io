  1. The circumference, \(C\text{,}\) of a circle varies directly with its radius, \(r\text{,}\) because

    \begin{equation*} C = 2\pi r \end{equation*}
    The constant of variation is \(2\pi\text{,}\) or about 6.28.

  2. The amount of interest, \(I\text{,}\) earned in one year on an account paying 7% simple interest, varies directly with the principal, \(P\text{,}\) invested, because

    \begin{equation*} I = 0.07P \end{equation*}
