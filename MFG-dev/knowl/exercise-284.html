Exercise 31.

A temporary typist's paycheck (before deductions) is given, in dollars, by \(S = 8t\text{,}\) where \(t\) is the number of hours she worked.

  1. Make a table of values for the function.

    \(t\) \(4\) \(8\) \(20\) \(40\)
    \(S\) \(\hphantom{0000} \) \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\)

  2. Graph the function.

  3. Using two points on the graph, compute the slope \(\dfrac{\Delta S}{\Delta t}\text{,}\) including units.

  4. What does the slope tell us about the typist's paycheck?
