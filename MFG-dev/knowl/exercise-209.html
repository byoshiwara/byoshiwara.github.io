  1. Use the graph to find all values of \(x\) for which
    1. \(\displaystyle y=12\)

    3. \(\displaystyle y\lt 12\)

    1. \(\displaystyle -2x + 6=12\)
    2. \(\displaystyle -2x + 6\gt 12\)
    3. \(\displaystyle -2x + 6\lt 12\)
  3. Explain why your answers to parts (a) and (b) are the same.
