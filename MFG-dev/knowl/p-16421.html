Consider the numerical coefficients and the powers of each variable separately. Use the second law of exponents to simplify each quotient of powers.

\begin{align*} \frac{3x^2 y^4}{6x^3 y} \amp = \frac{3}{6}\cdot \frac{x^2}{x^3} \cdot \frac{y^4}{y}\amp\amp\text{Subtract exponents.}\\ \amp = \frac{1}{2}\cdot \frac{1}{x^{3-2}} \cdot y^{4-1}\\ \amp = \frac{1}{2}\cdot \frac{1}{x} \cdot y^3=\frac{y^3}{2x} \end{align*} in-context
