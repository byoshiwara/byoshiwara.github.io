Quarterly means "4 times a year,"" so we use the formula for compound interest with \(P = 1000\text{,}\) \(r = 0.04\text{,}\) \(n = 4\text{,}\) and \(t = 5\text{.}\) \begin{align*} A(5) \amp = 1000\left(1 + \frac{0.04}{4}\right)^{4(5)} \\ \amp = 1000(1.01)^{20} = 1220.19 \end{align*} If interest is compounded quarterly, the balance in the account after \(5\) years is $\(1220.19\text{.}\)
