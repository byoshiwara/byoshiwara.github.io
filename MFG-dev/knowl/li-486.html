Item 1.62.a.

If we increase the radius by \(h\) centimeters, what will the new volume be?

  • \(V(10)+h=\frac{4}{3}\pi(10^3)+h \) cu. cm

  • \(V(10)+h=\frac{4}{3}\pi(10^{3+h}) \) cu. cm

  • \(V(10+h)=\frac{4}{3}\pi\cdot 10^3+h \) cu. cm

  • \(V(10+h)=\frac{4}{3}\pi(10+h)^3 \) cu. cm
