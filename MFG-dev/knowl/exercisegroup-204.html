Exercise Group 19–22.

In Problems 19–22, for the given function, evaluate each pair of expressions. Are they equivalent?

19.

\(f (x) = 3(5^x )\)

  1. \(f (a + 2)\) and \(9f (a)\)

  2. \(f (2a)\) and \(2 f (a)\)

20.

\(g(x) = 1.8^x\)

  1. \(g(h + 3)\) and \(g(h) g(3)\)

  2. \(g(2h)\) and \([g(h)]^2 \)

21.

\(P(t) = 8^t\)

  1. \(P(w)-P(z) \) and \(P(w-z) \)

  2. \(P(-x) \) and \(\dfrac{1}{P(x)} \)

22.

\(Q(t)=5(0.2)^t \)

  1. \(Q(b-1)\) and \(5Q(b) \)

  2. \(Q(a)Q(b) \) and \(5Q(a+b) \)
