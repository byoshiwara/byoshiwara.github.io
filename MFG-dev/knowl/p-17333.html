Francine wants to fence a rectangular area of \(3200\) square feet to grow vegetables for her family of three.

  1. Express the length of the garden as a function of its width.

  2. Express the perimeter, \(P\text{,}\) of the garden as a function of its width.

  3. Graph your function for perimeter and find the coordinates of the lowest point on the graph. Interpret those coordinates in the context of the problem.

  4. Francine has \(240\) feet of chain link to make a fence for the garden, and she would like to know what the width of the garden should be. Write an equation that describes this situation.

  5. Solve your equation and find the dimensions of the garden.
