A smart television set loses \(30\%\) of its value every \(2\) years.
  1. Write a function for the value of a television set \(t\) years after it was purchased if it cost \(\$700\) originally.
  2. Use your calculator to graph your function on the interval \(0 \le t\le 20\text{.}\)
  3. How long will it be before a \(\$700\) television set depreciates to \(\$343\text{?}\) Use algebraic methods to find your answer, and verify it on your graph.
