Solution 8.36.1.

To eliminate \(y\) from Equations (1) and (2), we multiply Equation (1) by \(-2\) and add the result to Equation (2).

\(-6x\) \(-\) \(2y\) \(+\) \(4z\) \(=\) \(-2\)
\(6x\) \(+\) \(2y\) \(-\) \(4z\) \(=\) \(5\)
\(0x\) \(+\) \(0y\) \(+\) \(0z\) \(=\) \(3\)

Since the resulting equation has no solution, the system is inconsistent.
