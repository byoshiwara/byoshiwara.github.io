Checkpoint 7.77. QuickCheck 2.

The fraction \(\dfrac{x^2-1}{x^2-4}\)
  • can be reduced to \(\dfrac{1}{4}\text{.}\)
  • is undefined at \(x=1\) and \(x=-1\text{.}\)
  • is equivalent to \(\dfrac{(x-1)^2}{(x-2)^2}\text{.}\)
  • is equal to zero at \(x=1\) and \(x=-1\text{.}\)
