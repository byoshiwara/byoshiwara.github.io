Exercise 5.
The owner of a movie theater needs to bring in $\(1000\) at each screening in order to stay in business. He sells adult tickets at $\(5\) apiece and children’s tickets at $\(2\) each.
  1. Write an equation that relates the number of adult tickets, \(A\text{,}\) he must sell and the number of children’s tickets, \(C\text{.}\)
  2. Find the intercepts and graph the equation by hand.
  3. If the owner sells \(120\) adult tickets, how many children’s tickets must he sell?
  4. What is the significance of the intercepts to the sale of tickets?
