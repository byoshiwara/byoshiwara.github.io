Remark A.26 Calculator Tip

On a scientific or a graphing calculator, we can enter the expression from Example A.25 just as it is written:

\begin{equation*} 200 + 0.09 ~ \boxed{\times}~ 350 ~\boxed{\text{ENTER}} \end{equation*}

The calculator will perform the operations in the correct order—multiplication first.
