Item b.

Suppose that the rate at which an animal catches its prey is proportional to the number of prey available, or \(r_c = ax\text{,}\) where \(a\) is a constant and \(x\) is the number of available prey. The rate at which it handles and eats the prey is constant, \(r_h = b\text{.}\) Write expressions for \(T_c\) and \(T_h\text{,}\) the times for catching and handling \(N\) prey.
