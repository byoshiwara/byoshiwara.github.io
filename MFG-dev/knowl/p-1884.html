The horizontal change \(\Delta x\) is half the difference between the sides of the base and of the top platform: \(\Delta x=\frac{1}{2}(55-19.5)= 17.75\text{,}\) so the slope is \(m=\dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}= \dfrac{24}{17.75} \approx {1.35211}\text{,}\) which is greater than 1.28. Kukulcan is steeper.
