Item a.

Because the general term, \(m^2\text{,}\) is neither linear nor exponential, we know that the series is not arithmetic or geometric. However, we can expand the series and evaluate it directly.

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] \displaystyle{\sum_{m=1}^{5} m^2}\amp = 1^2+2^2+3^2+4^2+5^2\\ \amp = 1+4+9+16+25=55\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
