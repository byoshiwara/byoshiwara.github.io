Exercise Group 25–30.

For Problems 25–30,

  1. Use a calculator or computer to graph the function in the suggested window.

  2. Use your graph to find two solutions for the given equation. (See Section 1.3 to review graphical solution of equations.)

  3. Check your solutions algebraically, using mental arithmetic.

25.

  1. \(y=\dfrac{1}{4}x^2 \)

    \begin{align*} {\text{Xmin}} \amp = -15 \amp\amp {\text{Xmax}} = 15\\ {\text{Ymin}} \amp = -10 \amp\amp {\text{Ymax}} = 40 \end{align*}

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{1}{4}x^2=36 \)

26.

  1. \(y=8x^2 \)

    \begin{align*} {\text{Xmin}} \amp = -15 \amp\amp {\text{Xmax}} = 15\\ {\text{Ymin}} \amp = -50 \amp\amp {\text{Ymax}} = 450 \end{align*}

  2. \(\displaystyle 8x^2=392 \)

27.

  1. \(y=(x-5)^2 \)

    \begin{align*} {\text{Xmin}} \amp = -5 \amp\amp {\text{Xmax}} = 15\\ {\text{Ymin}} \amp = -5 \amp\amp {\text{Ymax}} = 25 \end{align*}

  2. \(\displaystyle (x-5)^2=16 \)

28.

  1. \(y=(x+2)^2 \)

    \begin{align*} {\text{Xmin}} \amp = -10 \amp\amp {\text{Xmax}} = 10\\ {\text{Ymin}} \amp = -2 \amp\amp {\text{Ymax}} = 12 \end{align*}

  2. \(\displaystyle (x+2)^2=9 \)

29.

  1. \(y=3(x-4)^2 \)

    \begin{align*} {\text{Xmin}} \amp = -5 \amp\amp {\text{Xmax}} = 15\\ {\text{Ymin}} \amp = -20 \amp\amp {\text{Ymax}} = 130 \end{align*}

  2. \(\displaystyle 3(x-4)^2=108 \)

30.

  1. \(y=\dfrac{1}{2} (x+3)^2 \)

    \begin{align*} {\text{Xmin}} \amp = -15 \amp\amp {\text{Xmax}} = 5\\ {\text{Ymin}} \amp = -5 \amp\amp {\text{Ymax}} = 15 \end{align*}

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{1}{2} (x+3)^2=8 \)
