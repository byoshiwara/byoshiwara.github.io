Exercise 45

The intensity of illumination, \(I\text{,}\) from a lamp varies inversely with the square of your distance, \(d\text{,}\) from the lamp.

  1. If you double your distance from a reading lamp, what happens to the illumination?

  2. If you triple the distance, what happens to the illumination?

  3. If you increase the distance by \(25\%\text{,}\) what happens to the illumination?
