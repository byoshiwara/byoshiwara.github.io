A vegetarian recipe calls for \(32\) ounces of a combination of tofu and tempeh. Tofu provides \(2\) grams of protein per ounce and tempeh provides \(1.6\) grams of protein per ounce. Graham would like the dish to provide at least \(56\) grams of protein.
  1. Write a system of inequalities for the amount of tofu and the amount of tempeh for the recipe and graph the solutions.
  2. Suppose that tofu costs \(12\)¢ per ounce and tempeh costs \(16\)¢ per ounce. What is the least expensive combination of tofu and tempeh Graham can use for the recipe? How much will it cost?
