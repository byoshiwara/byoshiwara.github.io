Now, \(10^{1.5} = 10\sqrt{10}\text{,}\) or approximately \(31.62\text{.}\) Note how equal increments of \(0.5\) on the log scale correspond to equal factors of \(10^{0.5}\) in the values plotted:
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}[t] 10 \times 3.162 \amp = 31.62~~~~ \text{ and }~~~~ 31.62 \times 3.162 = 100\\ 10^1 \times 10^{0.5} \amp = 101.5~~~~ \text{ and }~~~~ 101.5 \times 10^{0.5} = 10^2 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
