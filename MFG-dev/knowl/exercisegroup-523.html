Exercise Group 29–32.

For Problems 29–32, simplify by applying the laws of exponents.

29.

  1. \(\displaystyle \left(\dfrac{-2x}{3y^2} \right)^3 \)

  2. \(\displaystyle \left(\dfrac{-x^2}{2y} \right)^4 \)

30.

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{(4x)^3}{(-2x^2)^2} \)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{(5x)^2}{(-3x^2)^3} \)

31.

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{(xy)^2(-x^2y)^3}{(x^2y^2)^2} \)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{(-x^2)(-x^2)^4}{(x^2)^3} \)

32.

  1. \(\displaystyle \left(\dfrac{-2x}{y^2}\right)\left(\dfrac{y^2}{3x} \right)^2 \)

  2. \(\displaystyle \left(\dfrac{x^2 z}{2} \right)^3 \left(\dfrac{-2}{x^2z} \right)^3 \)
