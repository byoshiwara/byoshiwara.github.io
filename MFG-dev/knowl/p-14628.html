  1. We multiply each side of Equation (2) by \(3\text{,}\) and each side of Equation (3) by \(2\text{,}\) to obtain the equivalent system

    \(x\) \(+\) \(2y\) \(-\) \(z\) \(=\) \(-3\) \(\hphantom{blank}\) \((1)\)
