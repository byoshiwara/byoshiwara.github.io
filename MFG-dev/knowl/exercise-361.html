Exercise 30.

European shoe sizes are scaled differently than American shoe sizes. The table shows the European equivalents for various American shoe sizes.

American shoe size \(5.5\) \(6.5\) \(7.5\) \(8.5\)
European shoe size \(37\) \(38\) \(39\) \(40\)

  1. Plot the data and draw a line through the data points.

  2. Calculate the slope of your line. Estimate the \(y\)-intercept from the graph.

  3. Find an equation that gives the European shoe size in terms of American shoe size.
