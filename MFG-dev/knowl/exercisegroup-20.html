The figure shows the graph of \(f(x) = \dfrac{30\sqrt{x}}{1 + x}\text{.}\)

  1. Solve \(\dfrac{30\sqrt{x}}{1 + x} = 15\)

27

The figure shows a graph of \(B = \dfrac{1}{3}p^3 - 3p + 2\text{.}\)

  1. Solve \(\dfrac{1}{3}p^3 - 3p + 2 = 6\)

  2. Solve \(\dfrac{1}{3}p^3 - 3p + 2=5\)

  3. Solve \(\dfrac{1}{3}p^3 - 3p + 2\lt 1\)

  4. What range of values does \(B\) have for \(p\) between \(-2.5\) and \(0.5\text{?}\)

  5. For what values of \(p\) is \(B\) increasing?

28

The figure shows a graph of \(H = t^3 - 4t^2 - 4t + 12\text{.}\)

  1. Solve \(t^3 - 4t^2 - 4t + 12 = -4\)

  2. Solve \(t^3 - 4t^2 - 4t + 12=16\)

  3. Solve \(t^3 - 4t^2 - 4t + 12\gt 6\)

  4. Estimate the horizontal and vertical intercepts of the graph.

  5. For what values of \(t\) is \(H\) increasing?

29

The figure shows a graph of \(M = g(q)\text{.}\)

  1. Find all values of \(q\) for which

    1. \(g(q) = 0\)

    2. \(g(q) = 16\)

    3. \(g(q)\lt 6\)

  2. For what values of \(q\) is \(g(q)\) increasing?

  1. Find all values of \(q\) for which

    1. \(f (t) = 3\)

    2. \(f (t)\gt 4.5\)

    3. \(2\le f (t)\le 4\)

  2. For what values of \(t\) is \(f (t)\) decreasing?
