Note that \(a = 2\) and that \(2 \gt 0\text{,}\) so the parabola opens upward. We can find the \(x\)-intercepts of the graph by setting \(y\) equal to zero: \begin{align*} 0 \amp= 2x^2 + 8x\\ \amp= 2x(x + 4) \end{align*} The solutions of this equation are \(0\) and \(-4\text{,}\) so the \(x\)-intercepts are the points \((0, 0)\) and \((-4, 0)\text{.}\)
