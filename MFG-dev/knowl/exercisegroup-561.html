Use graph paper for Problems 27-30.

27.

  1. Put the equation \(x-2y=5\) into slope-intercept form, and graph the equation.

  2. What is the slope of any line that is parallel to \(x-2y=5\text{?}\)

  3. On your graph for part (a), sketch by hand a line that is parallel to \(x-2y=5\) and passes through the point \((2,-1)\text{.}\)

  4. Use the point-slope formula to write an equation for the line that is parallel to the graph of \(x-2y=5\) and passes through the point \((2,-1)\text{.}\)
