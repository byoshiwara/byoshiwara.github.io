If you are flying in an airplane at an altitude of \(h\) miles, on a clear day you can see a distance of \(d\) miles to the horizon, where
\begin{equation*} d =\sqrt{7920h} \end{equation*}
  1. Write the formula for \(d\) as a power function in the form \(f (x) = kx^p\text{.}\)
  2. Choose a reasonable domain for the function \(d = f (h)\) and graph the function.
  3. At what altitude will you be able to see for a distance of \(100\) miles? How high is that in feet?
