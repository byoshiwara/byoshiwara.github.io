Exercise 20.

A box is made from a square piece of cardboard by cutting 2-inch squares from each corner and turning up the edges.

  1. If the piece of cardboard is \(x\) inches square, write expressions for the length, width and height of the box. Then write an expression for the volume \(V\) of the box in terms of \(x\text{.}\)
  2. Graph your expression for the volume on your calculator. What is the smallest value of \(x\) that makes sense for this problem? Why?
  3. Write and solve a quadratic equation to answer the question: What size cardboard do you need to make a box with volume 50 cubic inches?
