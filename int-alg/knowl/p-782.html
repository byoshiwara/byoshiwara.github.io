In the definition of slope,

\begin{equation*} \Delta x~ \text{ is}~ \begin{cases} \text{positive if }x\text{ increases} \amp \text{(we move to the right)}\\ \text{negative if }x\text{ decreases} \amp \text{(we move to the left)} \end{cases} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \Delta y~ \text{ is}~ \begin{cases} \text{positive if }y\text{ increases} \amp \text{(we move up)}\\ \text{negative if }y\text{ decreases} \amp \text{(we move down)}\hphantom{to left} \end{cases} \end{equation*}
