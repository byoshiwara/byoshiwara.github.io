Exercise 14.

The scatterplot shows the weights in pounds and the heights in inches of a team of distance runners.

  1. Use a straightedge to draw a line that fits the data.

  2. Use your line to predict the weight of a 65-inch tall runner and the weight of a 71-inch tall runner.

  3. Use your answers from part (b) to approximate the equation of your regression line.

  4. Use your answer from part(c) to predict the weight of a runner who is 68 inches tall.
