Note 7.1.

Look carefully at the difference between a power function, \(f(x) = kx^p\text{,}\) and this new exponential growth function, \(P(t)=P_0 b^t\text{.}\) The power function has the variable as its base, and the exponent is a constant. In the exponential growth function, the base is a constant and the exponent is a variable.
