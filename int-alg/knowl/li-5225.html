Item 6.41.c.

Write the formula for \(f(x)\) with a decimal exponent, and with radical notation.

\(f(x)\) is

  • \(\displaystyle x^{-2} \)

  • \(\displaystyle x^{0.5} \)

  • \(\displaystyle x^{-0.5} \)

and also, \(f(x)\) is

  • \(\displaystyle \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x}} \)

  • \(\displaystyle -\sqrt{x/2}\)

  • \(\displaystyle \dfrac{-\sqrt{x}}{2}\)
