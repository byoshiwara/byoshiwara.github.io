GeoGebra displays "built-up" fractions like \(\dfrac{1}{2} \text{.}\) Once we enter the numerator and select the ÷ key, the cursor is in the denominator and will stay there until we use an arrow key on the keyboard to move the cursor outside the fraction, or until we press ⮠. The arrow keys on the virtual keyboard are shown in the keyboard image A.1.
