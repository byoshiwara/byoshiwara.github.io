First we factor the numerator and denominator. Then we divide numerator and denominator by any common factors.

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{4x+2}{4} = \dfrac{\cancel{2}(2x+1)}{\cancel{2}(2)} = \dfrac{2x+1}{2}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{9x^2+3}{6x+3} = \dfrac{\cancel{3}(3x^2+1)}{\cancel{3}(2x+1)} = \dfrac{3x^2+1}{2x+1}\)
