Exercise 12.

On October 31, Betty and Paul fill their 250-gallon oil tank for their heater. Beginning in November they use an average of 15 gallons of oil per week.

  1. Complete the table of values for the amount of oil, \(A\text{,}\) left in the tank after \(w\) weeks.

    \(w\) (weeks) \(0\) \(4\) \(8\) \(12\) \(16\)
    \(A\) (gallons)

  2. Write an equation that expresses the amount of oil, \(A\text{,}\) in terms of the number of weeks, \(w\text{,}\) since October 31.

  3. Graph the equation.

  4. How much did the amount of fuel oil in the tank decrease between the third week and the eighth week? Illustrate this amount on the graph.

  5. During which weeks will the tank contain more than 175 gallons of fuel oil? Illustrate on the graph.

  6. Write and solve an inequality to verify your answer to part (e).
