Solution 9.4.18.1.

  1. A degenerate conic can be a point, a line, a pair of lines, or no graph at all.
  2. The coefficients \(D\text{,}\) \(E\text{,}\) and \(F\) determine the position of the conic.
  3. The graph is a parabola if one of \(A\) or \(C\) is zero.
  4. If \(A = C\) (and \(B=0\)), the graph is a circle.
