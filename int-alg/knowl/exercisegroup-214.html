Exercise Group 1–4.

In Problems 1–4, use the laws of exponents to simplify.

1.

  1. \(\displaystyle 3^x \, 3^4\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (3^x)^4\)

  3. \(\displaystyle 3^x \, 4^x\)

2.

  1. \(\displaystyle 8^x \, 8^x\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 8^{x+2} \, 8^{x-1} \)

  3. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{8^{2x}}{8^x} \)

3.

  1. \(\displaystyle b^{-4t} \, b^{2t} \)

  2. \(\displaystyle (b^t)^{1/2} \)

  3. \(\displaystyle b^{t-1} \, b^{1-t} \)

4.

  1. \(\displaystyle b^{t/2} \, b^{t/2} \)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{b^{2t}}{b} \)

  3. \(\displaystyle b^{1/t} \, b^t \)
