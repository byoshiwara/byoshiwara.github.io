Exercise Group 27–28.
For Problems 27–28,
  1. Find the break-even points by solving a system of equations.
  2. Graph the equations for revenue and cost in the same window and verify your solutions on the graph.
  3. Use the fact that
    \begin{gather*} \blert{\text{Profit} = \text{Revenue} - \text{Cost}} \end{gather*}
    to find the value of \(x\) for which profit is maximum.
