Exercise 29.

From 1950 to 2000, the population of the state of Nevada increased according to the formula

\begin{equation*} P(t) = 162,000 (10)^{0.02191t} \end{equation*}

where \(t\) is measured in years since \(1950\text{.}\)

  1. What was the population in \(2000\text{?}\)

  2. What was the annual percent growth rate from 1950 to 2000?

  3. According to this model, when would the population of Nevada reach \(3,000,000\text{?}\) (In fact the population of Nevada was 3,139,658 in 2020.)
