Formulas for Volume and Surface Area.
  1. Sphere \(~~~~~~V=\dfrac{4}{3}\pi r^3~~~~~~S=4\pi r^2\)
  2. Cylinder \(~~~~~~V=\pi r^2h~~~~~~S=2\pi r^2+2 \pi rh\)
  3. Cone \(~~~~~~V=\dfrac{1}{3}\pi r^2h~~~~~~S=\pi r^2+\pi rs\)
  4. Square Pyramid \(~~~~~~V=\dfrac{1}{3}s^2h\)
