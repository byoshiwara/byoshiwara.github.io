Example 10.2.5.

Plot the integer values 2 through 9 and 20 through 90 on a log scale.

Solution.

We compute the logarithm of each integer value.

\(x\) \(20\) \(30\) \(40\) \(50\) \(60\) \(70\) \(80\) \(90\)
\(\log {(x)}\) \(1.301\) \(1.477\) \(1.602\) \(1.699\) \(1.778\) \(1.845\) \(1.903\) \(1.954\)
