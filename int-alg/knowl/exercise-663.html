Exercise 20.

A travel agent offers a group rate of $2400 per person for a week in London if 16 people sign up for the tour. For each additional person who signs up, the price per person is reduced by $100.

  1. Let \(x\) represent the number of additional people who sign up. Write expressions for the number of people signed up, the price per person, and the total revenue.
  2. How many people must sign up for the tour in order for the travel agent to maximize her revenue?
