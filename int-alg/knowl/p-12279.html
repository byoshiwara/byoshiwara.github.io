Step 3: We subtract the resulting like fractions to obtain

\begin{align*} \dfrac{x+2}{6}-\dfrac{x-1}{15} \amp = \dfrac{5x+10}{30} - \dfrac{2x-2}{30} \amp \amp \glert{\text{Combine the numerators.}}\\ \amp = \dfrac{(5x+10) - (2x-2)}{30} \amp \amp \glert{\text{Simplify the numerator.}}\\ \amp = \dfrac{5x+10-2x+2}{30} = \dfrac{3x+12}{30} \end{align*}
