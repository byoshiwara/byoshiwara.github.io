Exercise Group 18–20.
For Problems 18–20, use the laws of exponents to simplify and write without negative exponents.
18.
  1. \(\displaystyle a^{-3} \cdot a^8\)
  2. \(\displaystyle 5^{-4} \cdot 5^{-3}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{p^{-7}}{p^{-4}}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \left(7^{-2}\right)^5\)
19.
  1. \(\displaystyle (4x^{-5})(5x^2)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{3u^{-3}}{9u^9}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{5^6t^0}{5^{-2}t^{-1}}\)
20.
  1. \(\displaystyle \left(3x^{-2}y^3\right)^{-2}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \left(\dfrac{6a^{-3}}{b^2}\right)^{-2}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{5h^{-3}\left(h^4\right)^{-2}}{6h^{-5}}\)
