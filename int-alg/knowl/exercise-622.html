Exercise 16.

  1. What is the sum of the two solutions of the quadratic equation \(~ax^2 + bx + c = 0~\text{?}\) (Hint: The two solutions are given by the quadratic formula.)

  2. What is the product of the two solutions of the quadratic equation \(~ax^2 + bx + c = 0~\text{?}\) (Hint: Do not try to multiply the two solutions given by the quadratic formula! Think about the factored form of the equation.)
