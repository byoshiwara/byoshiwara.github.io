Standard Form for a Circle.

The equation for a circle of radius \(r\) centered at the point \((h,k)\) is

\begin{gather*} \blert{(x-h)^2+(y-k)^2=r^2} \end{gather*}

Central Ellipse.

The equation of an ellipse centered at the origin is

\begin{gather*} \blert{\frac{x^2}{a^2}+\frac{y^2}{b^2}=1} \end{gather*}
