Technology 4.49. Solving an Inequality With a Graphing Calculator.

You can use your graphing calculator to solve the problem in Example 4.46. Graph the two functions

\begin{align*} Y_1 \amp= 256X - 16X^2\\ Y_2 \amp= 800 \end{align*}

on the same screen. Use WINDOW settings to match the graph in Example 4.46.

Then use the intersect feature to find the points where the two graphs intersect, at about \(x=4.26\) and \(x=11.74\text{.}\) Both these points \(y\)-coordinate \(800\text{,}\) and between the points the parabola is above the line, so \(h \gt 800\) when \(4.26 \lt t \lt 11.74\text{.}\)
