\begin{align*} 0 \amp= ax^2 + bx \amp\amp \glert{\text{Factor.}} \\ \amp= x(ax + b) \amp\amp \glert{\text{Set each factor equal to zero.}} \\ x \amp=0 ~~~\text{or}~~~ ax + b = 0 \amp\amp \glert{\text{Solve for}~x.} \\ x \amp=0 ~~~\text{or}~~~ x =\frac{-b}{a} \end{align*}

The \(x\)-intercepts are the points \((0, 0)\) and \((\dfrac{-b}{a}, 0)\text{.}\)
