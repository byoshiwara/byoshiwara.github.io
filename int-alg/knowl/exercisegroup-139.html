Exercise Group 21–26.

For Problems 21–26, graph the functions in the same window on your calculator. Describe how the graphs in parts (b) and (c) are different from the basic graph.

21.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(x)=x^3\)

  2. \(\displaystyle g(x)=x^3-2\)

  3. \(\displaystyle h(x)=x^3+1\)

22.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(x)=\abs{x}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle g(x)=\abs{x-2}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle h(x)=\abs{x+1}\)

23.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(x)=\dfrac{1}{x}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle g(x)=\dfrac{1}{x+1.5}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle h(x)=\dfrac{1}{x-1}\)

24.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(x)=\dfrac{1}{x^2}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle g(x)=\dfrac{1}{x^2}+2\)

  3. \(\displaystyle h(x)=\dfrac{1}{x^2}-1\)

25.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(x)=\sqrt{x}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle g(x)=-\sqrt{x}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle h(x)=\sqrt{-x}\)

26.

  1. \(\displaystyle f(x)=\sqrt[3]{x}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle g(x)=-\sqrt[3]{x}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle h(x)=\sqrt[3]{-x}\)
