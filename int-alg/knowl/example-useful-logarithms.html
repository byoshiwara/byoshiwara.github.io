Here are some more examples. Once again, we are looking for the exponent on the given base \(b\) to get \(x\text{.}\)
  1. \(\log_{2}{(2)} = 1~~\) because \(~~2^1=2\)
  2. \(\log_{5}{(1)} = 0~~\) because \(~~5^0=1\)
  3. \(\log_{3}{(3^4)} = 4~~\) because, well, \(~3^4=3^4\)
