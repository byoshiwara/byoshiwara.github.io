Item 6.45.b.

How do we solve the equation \(~\sqrt[6]{x} = 12\text{?}\)

  • Raise each side to the power 6.

  • Raise each side to the power \(\frac{1}{6}\text{.}\)

  • Multiply both sides by 6.

  • Multiply both sides by \(\frac{1}{6} \text{.}\)
