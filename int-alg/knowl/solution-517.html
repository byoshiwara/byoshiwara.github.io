Solution 8.39.1.

First, we factor each numerator and denominator. Then we divide numerator and denominator by any common factors.

\begin{align*} \dfrac{2x-4}{3x+6} \cdot \dfrac{6x+9}{x-2} \amp = \dfrac{2\cancel{(x-2)}}{\cancel{3}(x+2)} \cdot \dfrac{\cancel{3}(2x+3)}{\cancel{x-2}}\\ \amp = \dfrac{2(2x+3)}{x+2}~~~~\text{or}~~~~\dfrac{4x+6}{x+2} \end{align*}
