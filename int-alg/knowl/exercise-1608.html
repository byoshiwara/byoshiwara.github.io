Exercise 50.

The number of compact discs produced each year by Delta Discs is given by the function

\begin{equation*} N(t)=8000\cdot 3^{t/4} \end{equation*}

where \(t\) is the number of years since discs were introduced in 1980.

  1. How many discs did Delta produce in 1989?

  2. In what year did Delta first produce over 2 million discs?
