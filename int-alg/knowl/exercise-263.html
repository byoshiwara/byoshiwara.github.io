Exercise 14.
The Bread Alone Bakery has a daily overhead of $90. It costs $0.60 to bake each loaf of bread, and the bread sells for $1.50 per loaf.
  1. Write an equation for the cost \(C\) in terms of the number of loaves, \(x\text{.}\)
  2. Write an equation for the revenue \(R\) in terms of the number of loaves, \(x\text{.}\)
  3. Graph the revenue, \(R\text{,}\) and cost, \(C\text{,}\) on the same set of axes. State the solution of the system.
  4. How many loaves must the bakery sell to break even on a given day?
