The Graph of \(y=x^2+c\).

Compared to the graph of \(y=x^2\text{,}\) the graph of \(y=x^2+c\)

  • is shifted upward by \(c\) units if \(c \gt 0\)
  • is shifted downward by \(c\) units if \(c \lt 0\)
