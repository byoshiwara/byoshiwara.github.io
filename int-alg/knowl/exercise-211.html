Exercise 30.

The population of Maple Rapids was 4800 in 2005 and had grown to 6780 by 2020. Assume that the population increases at a constant rate.

  1. Make a table of values showing two data points.

  2. Find a linear equation that expresses the population, \(P\text{,}\) of Maple Rapids in terms of the number of years, \(t\text{,}\) since 2005.

  3. State the slope of the line, including units, and explain its meaning in the context of the problem.
