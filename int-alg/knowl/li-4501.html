Item a.
Using the fact that \(9 = \blert{3^2}\text{,}\) we write each side of the equation as a power of \(3\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} 3^{x-2} \amp = \left(\blert{3^2}\right)^3 \amp \amp \blert{\text{Simplify the right side.}}\\ 3^{x-2} \amp = 3^6 \end{align*}
Now we equate the exponents to obtain
\begin{align*} x - 2 \amp = 6 \end{align*}
The solution is \(x=8\text{.}\)
