<<<<<<< HEAD <<<<<<< HEAD Item 19.

The loudness of a sound is measured in decibels, \(D\text{,}\) by

\begin{equation*} D=10 \log_{10}\left(\frac{I}{10^{-12}}\right) \end{equation*}

where \(I\) is the intensity of its sound waves (in watts per square meter).
