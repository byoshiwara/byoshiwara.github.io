Decide whether each statement is true or false.
  1. The graph of a general quadratic is a hyperbola if the coefficients of both the \(x^2\)-term and the \(y^2\)-term are negative.
  2. The slopes of the asymptotes of a hyperbola are \(\pm\dfrac{b}{a} \text{.}\)
  3. The asymptotes pass through the vertices of the hyperbola.
  4. We find the center of the hyperbola by completing the square.
