in-context ======= Item 2.
Degree of a Product.

The degree of a product of non-zero polynomials is the sum of the degrees of the factors. That is:

If \(P(x)\) has degree \(m\) and \(Q(x)\) has degree \(n\text{,}\) then their product \(P(x)Q(x)\) has degree \(m+n\text{.}\)
