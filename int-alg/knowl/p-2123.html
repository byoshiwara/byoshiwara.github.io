A business venture calculates its profit by subtracting its costs from its revenue, the amount of money it takes in from sales.

\begin{gather*} \blert{\text{Profit} = \text{Revenue} -\text{Cost}} \end{gather*}

Cost is usually calculated as the sum of fixed costs, or overhead, and variable costs,the cost of labor and materials to produce its product. Revenue is the product ofthe selling price per item times the number of items sold.
