Exercise 55.

The half-life of iodine-131 is approximately 8 days.

  1. If a sample initially contains \(N_0\) grams of iodine-131, how much will it contain after 8 days? How much will it contain after 16 days? After 32 days?

  2. Use your answers to part (a) to sketch a graph of \(N(t)\text{,}\) the amount of iodine-131 remaining, versus time. (Choose an arbitrary height for \(N_0\) on the vertical axis.)

  3. Calculate \(k\text{,}\) and hence find a decay law of the form \(N(t) = N_0e^{kt}\text{,}\) where \(k \lt 0\text{,}\) for iodine-131.
