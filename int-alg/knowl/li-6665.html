Item 7.77.c.

\(\begin{aligned}[t] 12(10)^{-0.06t}\amp=192 \\ \color{gray}{120^{-0.06t}}\amp=\color{gray}{192}\amp\amp \color{green}{\text{(Wrong!)}} \end{aligned}\)

Instead, the first step should be

  • \((\log 12)\left(\log 10^{-0.06t}\right)=\log 192\)

  • \(10^{-0.72t} = 192\)

  • \(10^{-0.06t} = \frac{192}{12}\)
