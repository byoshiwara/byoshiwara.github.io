We substitute 1000 for \(A(t)\) and solve the equation.
\begin{align*} 1000 \amp= 500 e^{0.08t}\amp\amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by 500.}}\\ 2 \amp = e^{0.08t}\amp\amp \blert{\text{Take natural log of both sides.}}\\ \ln 2 \amp= \ln e^{0.08t} = 0.08t\amp\amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by 0.08.}}\\ t \amp= \frac{\ln 2}{0.08}\approx 8.6643 \end{align*}
