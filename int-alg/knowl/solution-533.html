Solution A.7.34.1.
  1. First isolate the power to get \(2^x = 32\text{.}\) Then convert to logarithmic form as \(x = \log_2{(32)}\text{.}\) Because \(2^5 = 32\text{,}\) we can simplify the answer to \(x = 5\text{.}\)
  2. First isolate the power to get \(2^x = 30\text{.}\) Then convert to logarithmic form as \(x = \log_2{(30)}\text{.}\) This log is not easy to evaluate without a calculator.
