Activity 2.14. Like Terms and Parentheses.

  1. One angle of a triangle is three times the smallest angle, and the third angle is 20\(\degree\) greater than the smallest angle.

    1. If the smallest angle is \(x\text{,}\) write expressions for the other two angles.
    2. Write an expression for the sum of the three angles in terms of \(x\text{.}\)

  2. The StageLights theater group plans to sell T-shirts to raise money. It will cost them \(5x+60\) dollars to print \(x\) T-shirts, and they will sell the T-shirts at $12 each.

    1. Write and simplify an expression for their profit from selling \(x\) T-shirts.
    2. How many T-shirts must they sell in order to make a profit of $500?
