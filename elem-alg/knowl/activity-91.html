Activity 7.10. Special Products.

  1. Simplify each square.

    1. \(\displaystyle (-6h^6)^2\)
    2. \(\displaystyle (12st^8)^2\)

  2. Find a monomial whose square is given.

    1. \(\displaystyle 64b^6\)
    2. \(\displaystyle 169a^4b^{16}\)

  3. Use a formula to expand each product.

    1. \(\displaystyle (4-3t)^2\)
    2. \(\displaystyle (6s+2t)^2\)
    3. \(\displaystyle (5x^4+4)(5x^4-4)\)
