Exercise Group 17–26.

For Problems 17–26, evaluate the expression for the given values of the variables. Round to three decimal places any answers which are irrational numbers.

17.

\(a=3\)

  1. \(5-a^2\)
  2. \((5-a)^2\)
18.

\(a=-3\)

  1. \(5-a^2\)
  2. \((5-a)^2\)
19.

\(w=-2\)

  1. \(-w^2\)
  2. \((-w)^2\)
20.

\(w=-2\)

  1. \(\sqrt{-w}\)
  2. \(\sqrt[3]{-w}\)
21.

\(x=-4\)

  1. \(3-x^2\)
  2. \(3(-x)^2\)
22.

\(a=6,~b=-2\)

  1. \(\dfrac{3b^3}{6}-\dfrac{4-a^2}{8}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{(a-b)^2}{ab^2}\)
23.

\(x=13,~y=-5\)

  1. \(\sqrt{x^2-y^2}\)
  2. \((\sqrt{x}^2)+(\sqrt{-y}^2)\)
24.

\(a=2,~b=-3,~c=1\)

  1. \(\sqrt{b^2-4ac}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{-b+\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\)
25.

\(x=3,~y=4,~z=5\)

  1. \(\sqrt[3]{x^3+y^3+z^3}\)
  2. \(\sqrt[3]{x^3}+\sqrt[3]{y^3}+\sqrt[3]{z^3}\)
26.

\(m=3,~n=2\)

  1. \(m^3+n^3\)
  2. \((m+n)(m^2-mn+n^2)\)
