Exercise Group 13–14.

For Problems 13–14,

  1. Find the \(x\)- and \(y\)-intercepts of the parabola.
  2. Find the vertex of the parabola.
  3. Graph the pair of parabolas on the same grid and compare.
13.
  1. \(\displaystyle y=x^2-x-2\)
  2. \(\displaystyle y=-x^2+x+2\)
14.
  1. \(\displaystyle y=x^2-2x-15\)
  2. \(\displaystyle y=x^2+2x-15\)
