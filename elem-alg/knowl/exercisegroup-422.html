Exercise Group 18–24.

For Problems 18–22, reduce each fraction if possible, and select the correct response, (a) or (b).

18.

\(\dfrac{x+2}{y+2}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{x}{y}\)

  2. Cannot be reduced

19.

\(\dfrac{2x+4}{4}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{x+2}{2}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 2x\)

20.

\(\dfrac{y^2-1}{y-1}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle y+1\)

  2. \(\displaystyle y\)

21.

\(\dfrac{x^2+z^2}{x+z}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle x+z\)

  2. Cannot be reduced

22.

\(\dfrac{n^2+n}{n}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle n^2\)

  2. \(\displaystyle n+1\)

23.

\(\dfrac{z^2+2z+1}{z+1}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle z+1\)

  2. \(\displaystyle z^2+2\)

24.

\(\dfrac{m^2+4}{m+2}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle m+2\)

  2. Cannot be reduced
