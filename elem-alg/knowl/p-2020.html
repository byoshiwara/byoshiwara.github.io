Goodlife Insurance Company has $150,000 in fees from its clients to invest. They deposited part of the money into bonds that pay 6.5% annual interest and the rest into a mutual fund that pays 11.8% annual interest.

  1. Assign variables to the amount of money Goodlife deposited into each account, and write an equation about the sum of the deposits.
  2. Write expressions for the interest earned on each account after 1 year.
  3. Goodlife earned $12,930 interest in 1 year. Write an equation about that.
  4. Solve your system of equations to find out how much Goodlife invested in each account.
