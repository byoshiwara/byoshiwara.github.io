Exercise Group 48–50.

Mental Exercise: For Problems 48–50, decide which expressions are equivalent to the given fraction. Do not use pencil, paper, or calculator!

48.

\(\dfrac{2}{3}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{2+4}{3+4}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{2 \cdot 4}{3 \cdot 4}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{22}{33}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{2 \div 4}{3 \div 4}\)

49.

\(\dfrac{3}{5}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{3-x}{5-x}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{30+x}{x+50}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{30x}{50x}\)
