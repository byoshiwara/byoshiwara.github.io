Solution 3.9.1.

We apply the property of proportions and cross-multiply to get

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} 2.4x \amp = 1.5(8.4) \amp \amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by 2.4.}}\\ x \amp = \dfrac{1.5(8.4)}{2.4} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Simplify the right side.}}\\ x \amp = 5.25 \end{aligned} \end{equation*}

We can check the solution by substituting \(x=2.5\) into the original proportion.
