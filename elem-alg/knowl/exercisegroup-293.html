Exercise Group 17–26.

For Problems 17–26, evaluate the expression for the given values of the variables. Round to three decimal places any answers which are irrational numbers.

17.

\(a=3\)

  1. \(\displaystyle 5-a^2\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (5-a)^2\)

Answer.
18.

\(a=-3\)

  1. \(\displaystyle 5-a^2\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (5-a)^2\)

19.

\(w=-2\)

  1. \(\displaystyle -w^2\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (-w)^2\)

Answer.
20.

\(w=-2\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \sqrt{-w}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \sqrt[3]{-w}\)

21.

\(x=-4\)

  1. \(\displaystyle 3-x^2\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 3(-x)^2\)

Answer.
22.

\(a=6,~b=-2\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{3b^3}{6}-\dfrac{4-a^2}{8}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{(a-b)^2}{ab^2}\)

23.

\(x=13,~y=-5\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \sqrt{x^2-y^2}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (\sqrt{x}^2)+(\sqrt{-y}^2)\)

Answer.
24.

\(a=2,~b=-3,~c=1\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \sqrt{b^2-4ac}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{-b+\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\)

25.

\(x=3,~y=4,~z=5\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \sqrt[3]{x^3+y^3+z^3}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \sqrt[3]{x^3}+\sqrt[3]{y^3}+\sqrt[3]{z^3}\)

Answer.
26.

\(m=3,~n=2\)

  1. \(\displaystyle m^3+n^3\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (m+n)(m^2-mn+n^2)\)
