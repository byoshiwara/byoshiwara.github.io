The difference \(5-2\) is not the same as \(2-5\text{.}\) When we translate "\(a\) subtracted from \(b\text{,}\)" the order of the terms is important. \begin{align*} b-a~~~~\amp \text{means} \amp\amp a~~ \text{subtracted from}~~ b\\ x-7~~~~\amp \text{means} \amp\amp 7~~ \text{subtracted from}~~ x\\ \end{align*} The operation of subtraction is not commutative.
