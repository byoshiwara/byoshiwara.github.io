Solution 2.6.1.

We add the signed numbers \(7\) and \(-9\) and \(12\) and \(-6\text{,}\) in order from left to right.

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \blert{7-9}+12-6 \amp =\blert{-2}+12-6 \amp \amp \blert{\text{The sum of} ~7~ \text{and}~-9~\text{is}~-2.}\\ \amp =10-6=4 \amp \amp \blert{\text{The sum of}~-2~\text{and}~12~\text{is}~10.} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
