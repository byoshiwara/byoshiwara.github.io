Item 1.a.

Graph the line of slope \(\dfrac{-1}{2}\) that passes through the point \((-3,2)\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \blert{\text{Plot the point}~~(-3,2).}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \blert{\text{Use the slope to find a second point:} ~~~m =\dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \dfrac{-1}{2}}\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \blert{\text{Use the slope to find a third point:} ~~~m =\dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \dfrac{1}{-2}}\)
