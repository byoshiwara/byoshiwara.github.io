There are two points on the graph with \(p\)-coordinate 300. Those points have \(p\)-coordinates 10 and 30. If he charges $10 per hour, he will attract \(40-10=30\) clients, and if he charges $30 per hour, he will attract \(40-30=10\) clients. In either case, his revenue will be $300.
