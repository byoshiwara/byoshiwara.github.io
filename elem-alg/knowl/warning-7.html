Caution 2.7.

The following calculation is incorrect:

\begin{equation*} 16-\blert{9-4}+3~=~16-\blert{5}+3~=~11+3~=~14~~~~~~\alert {\leftarrow \text{Incorrect!}} \end{equation*}

We must perform additions and subtractions in order from left to right:

\begin{equation*} \blert{16-9}-4+3~=~\blert{7}-4+3~=~3+3~=~6 \end{equation*}
