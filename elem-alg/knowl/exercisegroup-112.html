Exercise Group 13–18.

  2. \(-2x=k\)

  1. \(x+4=7\)
  2. \(x+4=k\)
15.
  1. \(x-5=9\)
  2. \(x-5=k\)
16.
  1. \(2x+3=8\)
  2. \(2x+k=8\)
17.
  1. \(15-4x=3\)
  2. \(15-4x=k\)
18.
  1. \(9+3x=-1\)
  2. \(9+kx=-1\)
