We apply the property of proportions and cross-multiply to get

\begin{align*} 2.4x \amp = 1.5(8.4) \amp \amp \blert{\text{Divide both sides by 2.4.}}\\ x \amp = \dfrac{1.5(8.4)}{2.4} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Simplify the right side.}}\\ x \amp = 5.25 \end{align*}

We can check the solution by substituting \(x=5.25\) into the original proportion.
