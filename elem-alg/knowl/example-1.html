Example 1.1.

\(1900\) \(49\)
\(1910\) \(51\)
\(1920\) \(58\)
\(1930\) \(59\)
\(1940\) \(63\)
\(1950\) \(68\)
\(1960\) \(70\)
\(1970\) \(71\)
\(1980\) \(74\)
\(1990\) \(75\)

  1. To what age could people born in 1940 expect to live?
