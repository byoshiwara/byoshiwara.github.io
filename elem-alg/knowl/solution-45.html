Solution 2.28.1.

To solve for \(x\text{,}\) we first add 5 on each side of the inequality symbols.

\begin{alignat*}{2} -3 \amp \lt 2x-5 \amp\amp\le 6\\ \underline{\blert{+5}}\amp \quad\qquad\underline{\blert{+5}} \amp\amp ~~~~\underline{\blert{+5}}\\ -2 \amp \le 2x \amp\amp \le 11 \end{alignat*}

Next, to solve \(2 \lt 2x \le 11\text{,}\) we divide each side by 2.

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \dfrac{2}{\blert{2}} \amp \lt \dfrac{2x}{\blert{2}} \le \dfrac{11}{\blert{2}}\\ 1 \amp \lt x \le \dfrac{11}{2} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
