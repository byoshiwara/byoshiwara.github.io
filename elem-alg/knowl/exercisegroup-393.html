Exercise Group 31–33.

For Problems 31–33, combine the fractions.

31.
  1. \(\dfrac{2b}{a} \cdot \dfrac{4}{b}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{2b}{a} - \dfrac{4}{b}\)
  3. \(\dfrac{2b}{a} \div \dfrac{4}{b}\)
  4. \(\dfrac{2b}{a} + \dfrac{4}{b}\)
32.
  1. \(\dfrac{2y}{3x^2} \div \dfrac{6}{xy}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{2y}{3x^2} + \dfrac{6}{xy}\)
  3. \(\dfrac{2y}{3x^2} \cdot \dfrac{6}{xy}\)
  4. \(\dfrac{2y}{3x^2} - \dfrac{6}{xy}\)
33.
  1. \(\dfrac{3}{2+a} - \dfrac{2}{a^2-4}\)
  2. \(\dfrac{3}{2+a} \cdot \dfrac{2}{a^2-4}\)
  3. \(\dfrac{3}{2+a} + \dfrac{2}{a^2-4}\)
  4. \(\dfrac{3}{2+a} \div \dfrac{2}{a^2-4}\)
