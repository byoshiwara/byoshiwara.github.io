Example 5.1.

Compute the following powers.

  1. \(\displaystyle 6^2 = 6 \cdot 6 = 36\)

  2. \(\displaystyle 3^4 = 3 \cdot 3 \cdot 3 \cdot 3 = 81\)

  3. \(\displaystyle (\dfrac{2}{3})^3 = (\dfrac{2}{3})(\dfrac{2}{3})(\dfrac{2}{3}) = \dfrac{8}{27}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle 1.4^4 = 1.4(1.4)(1.4)(1.4) = 3.8416\)
