Some Useful Algebraic Formulas.
  1. distance \(=\) rate \(\times\) time \(~~~~~~d=rt\)
  2. profit \(=\) revenue \(-\) cost \(~~~~~~P=R-C\)
  3. interest \(=\) principal \(\times\) interest rate \(\times\) time \(~~~~~~I=Prt\)
  4. part \(=\) percentage rate \(\times\) whole \(~~~~~~P=rW\)
  5. average score \(= \dfrac{\text{sum of scores}}{\text{number of scores}}\) \(~~~~~~A=\dfrac{S}{n}\)
