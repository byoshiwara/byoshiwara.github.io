Exercise 9.

Gregory purchased stereo equipment on a monthly installment plan. After \(m\) months, Gregory still owes a balance of \(B\) dollars.

  1. What was the price of the stereo equipment?

  2. How much does Gregory pay each month?

  3. How much does Gregory owe after 6 months?

  4. How many monthly payments will Gregory make?

  5. Write an algebraic equation for \(B\) in terms of \(m\text{.}\)
