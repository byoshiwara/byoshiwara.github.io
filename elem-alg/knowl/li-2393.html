Item b.

In this quotient, the larger power occurs in the denominator.

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{a^4}{a^8} = \dfrac{a\cdot a\cdot a\cdot a}{a\cdot a\cdot a\cdot a\cdot a\cdot a\cdot a \cdot a} = \dfrac{1}{a^4} \end{equation*}

We see that we can subtract the exponent of the numerator from the exponent of the denominator. That is,

\begin{equation*} \dfrac{a^4}{a^8}=\dfrac{1} {a^{8-4}} = \dfrac{1}{a^4} \end{equation*}
