A sum of three or more terms can be added in any order. In the sums below, the parentheses tell us which part of the expression to simplify first. \begin{align*} (\blert{2} \amp \blert{+5})+8 \amp \text{and}~~~~~~~~~~2 \amp + (\blert{5+8})\\ \amp = \blert{7}+8=15 \amp \amp = 2+\blert{13}=15 \end{align*}
