Exercise Group 12–17.

For Problems 12–17, reduce the fraction if possible. If the fraction cannot be reduced, state the reason.

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{a \cdot 4}{a \cdot 5}\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \dfrac{z(-3)}{z(+9)}\)
