Exercise Group 13–18.

For Problems 13–18, solve each pair of equations. In part (b) of each problem, your answer will involve the constant \(k\text{.}\)

13.
  1. \(\displaystyle -2x=6\)
  2. \(\displaystyle -2x=k\)
14.
  1. \(\displaystyle x+4=7\)
  2. \(\displaystyle x+4=k\)
15.
  1. \(\displaystyle x-5=9\)
  2. \(\displaystyle x-5=k\)
16.
  1. \(\displaystyle 2x+3=8\)
  2. \(\displaystyle 2x+k=8\)
17.
  1. \(\displaystyle 15-4x=3\)
  2. \(\displaystyle 15-4x=k\)
18.
  1. \(\displaystyle 9+3x=-1\)
  2. \(\displaystyle 9+kx=-1\)
