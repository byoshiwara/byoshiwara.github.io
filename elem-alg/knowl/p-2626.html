  • To evaluate \(2x^3\text{,}\) we compute the power \(x^3\) first, and then the product, \(2 \cdot x^3\text{.}\)
  • To evaluate \((2x)^3\text{,}\) we compute the product \(2 \cdot x\) inside parentheses first, and then compute the power.
