There is no terminating decimal fraction that gives the exact value of \(\sqrt{5}\text{.}\) The decimal representation of an irrational number never ends, and does not repeat any pattern! The best we can do is round off the decimal form and give an approximate value. Nonetheless, an irrational number still has a precise location on the number line, just as a rational number does.
