First Law of Exponents

\begin{equation*} \blert{a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}} \end{equation*}

Second Law of Exponents

\begin{gather*} \blert{\dfrac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}~~~~(n \lt m)}\\ \blert{\dfrac{a^m}{a^n} = \dfrac{1}{a^{n-m}}~~~~(n \gt m)} \end{gather*}
