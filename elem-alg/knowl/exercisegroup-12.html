For the Exercises below,

  1. Choose the appropriate formula and write an algebraic expression.

  2. Evaluate the expression to answer the question.

1.

It cost Ariel $380 to buy supplies and advertising for her pet-sitting business.

  1. Write an expression for her profit from the business.

2.

Jamie cycled for \(2\frac{1}{2}\) hours this morning.

  1. Write an expression for the distance Jamie cycled.

  2. If Jamie's average speed was 9 miles per hour, how far did she cycle?

3.

SaveOurPark collected $2600 in donations from people in the neighborhood.

  1. Write an expression for the average amount that each person donated.

  2. If 142 people made a donation, what was the average donation?
